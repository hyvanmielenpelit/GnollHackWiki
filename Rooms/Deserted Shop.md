> 👉 **A Deserted Shop is a treacherous special room disguised as a standard shop, hiding a dangerous [[Gargantuan Mimic|/Monsters/Gargantuan Mimic]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Deserted Shop is a GnollHack-specific special room designed to trick unsuspecting adventurers. To the casual observer, it appears to be a normal shop, complete with the cityscape floor and a "shopkeeper" standing near the door. However, the shop is completely deserted, and the "shopkeeper" is actually a deadly [[Gargantuan Mimic|/Monsters/Gargantuan Mimic]] waiting to ambush you.

## 🗺️ Generation

Deserted shops can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the Dungeons of Doom. It mimics the exact structure of a real shop (a room with one door and no stairs).
