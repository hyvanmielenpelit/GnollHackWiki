> 👉 **A General store is a common shop that sells a wide variety of items.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A General store is the most common type of [[/Rooms/shop]] in the game. Unlike specialized shops, it does not focus on a single item category. Instead, it offers a diverse mix of items, including weapons, armor, food, potions, scrolls, and more. This makes it a great place to find a little bit of everything you might need for your adventure.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
General stores can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
