> 👉 **A Hardware store is a store that specializes in utility items and containers.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Hardware store is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] entirely dedicated to selling tools. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks 100% tools, making this the best place to find essential adventuring gear such as bags, chests, keys, lamps, lock picks, and musical instruments.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Hardware stores can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
