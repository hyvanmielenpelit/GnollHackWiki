> 👉 **A Rare books shop is a store that specializes in magical spellbooks.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Rare books shop is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] geared toward spellcasters and readers. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] focuses heavily on selling spellbooks (90%), supplemented by a small selection of magical scrolls (10%). 
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Rare books shops can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
