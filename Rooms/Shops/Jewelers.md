> 👉 **A Jewelers is a store that specializes in jewelry.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Jewelers is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] that deals in valuable and magical accessories. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] primarily sells rings (85%), along with a selection of gems (10%) and amulets (5%).
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Jewelers can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
