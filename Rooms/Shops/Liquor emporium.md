> 👉 **A Liquor emporium is a store that specializes in magical liquids.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Liquor emporium is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] entirely dedicated to selling potions. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks 100% potions, making this an ideal location to find healing, identify, or other useful magical concoctions, provided you have the gold to buy them.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Liquor emporiums can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
