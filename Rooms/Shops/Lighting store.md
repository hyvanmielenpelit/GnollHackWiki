> 👉 **A Lighting store is a unique store that specializes in lighting.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Lighting store is a unique specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] focused entirely on illumination. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks items like [[wax|/Items/Wax candle]] and [[tallow candles|/Items/Tallow candle]], [[brass lanterns|/Items/Brass lantern]], [[oil lamps|/Items/Oil lamp]], [[magic lamps|/Items/Magic lamp]], and [[torches|/Items/Torch]]. 
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Unlike most other shop types, a Lighting store does not generate randomly in the Dungeons of Doom (it has a 0% random generation probability). Instead, it appears exclusively as a guaranteed, unique shop—famously known as Izchak's lighting store—in [[/Dungeon/Mine Town]].
