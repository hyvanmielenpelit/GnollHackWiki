> 👉 **A Lighting store is a unique store that specializes in lighting.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Lighting store is a unique specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] focused entirely on illumination. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks items like wax and tallow candles, brass lanterns, oil lamps, magic lamps, and torches. 
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Unlike most other shop types, a Lighting store does not generate randomly in the Dungeons of Doom (it has a 0% random generation probability). Instead, it appears exclusively as a guaranteed, unique shop—famously known as Izchak's lighting store—in [[/Dungeon/Minetown]].
