> 👉 **A Health food store is a specialized shop that sells vegan and health-oriented provisions.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Health Food Store is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] focusing on healthy nourishment. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks mostly vegetarian food items (67%), supplemented by fruit juices, healing potions (including full and greater healing), scrolls of food detection, and the occasional lump of royal jelly or bag of the glutton.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Health food stores can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
