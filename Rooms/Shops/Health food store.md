> 👉 **A Health food store is a specialized shop that sells vegan and health-oriented provisions.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Health Food Store is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] focusing on healthy nourishment. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks mostly vegetarian food items (67%), supplemented by [[fruit juices|/Items/Potion of fruit juice]], [[healing potions|/Items/Potion of healing]] (including [[full|/Items/Potion of full healing]] and [[greater healing|/Items/Potion of greater healing]]), [[scrolls of food detection|/Items/Scroll of food detection]], and the occasional [[lump of royal jelly|/Items/Lump of royal jelly]] or [[bag of the glutton|/Items/Bag of the glutton]].
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Health food stores can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
