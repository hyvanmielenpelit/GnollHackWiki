> 👉 **An Antique weapons outlet is a store that specializes in offensive armaments.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
An Antique weapons outlet is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] that focuses on combat gear. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] stocks primarily weapons (about 90% of the inventory) with a small selection of armor pieces (10%). It is the perfect place for characters looking to upgrade their melee or ranged combat potential.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Antique weapons outlets can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
