> 👉 **A Used armor dealership is a store that specializes in selling protective gear.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Used armor dealership is a specialized [[/Rooms/shop]] that focuses heavily on defensive equipment. The [[/Monsters/shopkeeper]] here primarily sells various pieces of armor (which make up about 90% of the stock) along with a small selection of weapons (10%). It is an excellent place to upgrade your defensive capabilities.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Used armor dealerships can be generated randomly in place of a normal shop in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]]. They require a room with exactly one door and no stairs.
