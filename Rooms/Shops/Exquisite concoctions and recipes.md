> 👉 **An Exquisite concoctions and recipes shop is a unique store found on the Modron Plane, specializing in high-level potions and scrolls.**

## ℹ️ Description

The "exquisite concoctions and recipes" shop is a highly specialized, top-tier magic shop. Unlike standard potion or scroll shops, this store sells only the most powerful and sought-after magical consumables. It is tended by a peaceful [[Modron Quarton|/Monsters/Modron]] shopkeeper.

## 🗺️ Generation

This shop is a unique room type (specifically the Modron Shop) and has a 0% random generation probability. It generates exclusively on the [[/Dungeon/Plane of the Modron]], taking up a fixed region of the level. The shop is always lit and features parquet-style floors.

## 📦 Inventory

The shopkeeper sells premium scrolls and potions, with specific stock probabilities:
- [[/Items/Potion of full healing]] (15% chance)
- [[/Items/Potion of full energy]] (10% chance)
- [[/Items/Potion of greater regeneration]] (10% chance)
- [[/Items/Potion of lightning speed]] (15% chance)
- [[/Items/Scroll of conflict]] (15% chance)
- [[/Items/Scroll of taming]] (10% chance)
- [[/Items/Scroll of genocide]] (5% chance)
- Other random potions (2%) and scrolls (3%)
