> 👉 **An Artificer room is a specialized NPC room inhabited by an Artificer.**

## ℹ️ Description

An Artificer room is a GnollHack-specific NPC room. It serves as the workshop for an Artificer, a non-player character skilled in magical engineering and crafting.

## 🗺️ Generation

Artificer rooms are generated as a subtype of the generic NPC room. When an NPC room is generated, there is a chance it will be assigned the Artificer subtype, placing an Artificer NPC inside the room.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Services:** Artificers are highly knowledgeable about magical items and constructs. While their specific services can vary, they may provide ways to identify magical gear, recharge wands, or offer rare tools and mechanical items.
- **Interaction:** Approach the Artificer and interact with them (using the `#chat` command) to see what services or goods they are willing to provide.
- **Protection:** As with all peaceful NPCs, do not attack the Artificer without good reason, as they can defend themselves or you may lose access to their valuable services permanently.
