> 👉 **A Garden is a peaceful special room containing trees, herbs, and garden gnomes.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Garden is a unique GnollHack-specific special room designed as an indoor courtyard or arboretum. It is filled with trees and herbs, providing a natural respite from the dark dungeon. The garden is tended by [[garden gnomes|/Monsters/Gnome]], who can be found posing as statues.

## 🗺️ Generation

Gardens can be generated randomly as a special room. When a garden is generated, the room is populated with garden-variety trees and various herbs on the floor. 

The room is also home to [[garden gnomes|/Monsters/Gnome]] (`G`). Some of these gnomes may generate as statues wearing conical hats, blending in with the scenery until disturbed.
