> 👉 **A Garden is a peaceful special room containing trees, herbs, and garden gnomes.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Garden is a unique GnollHack-specific special room designed as an indoor courtyard or arboretum. It is filled with trees and herbs, providing a natural respite from the dark dungeon. The garden is tended by garden gnomes, who can sometimes be found posing as statues.

## 🗺️ Generation

Gardens can be generated randomly as a special room. When a garden is generated, the room is populated with garden-variety trees and various herbs on the floor. 

The room is also home to garden gnomes (`G`). Some of these gnomes may generate as statues wearing conical hats, blending in with the scenery until disturbed.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Foraging:** The herbs and trees make gardens an excellent place to forage for rare ingredients or food.
- **Statues:** Be cautious when interacting with the gnome statues, as they might actually be living garden gnomes in disguise. However, statues can also contain useful items if you choose to break them.
- **Line of Sight:** The trees in the garden block line of sight and movement, providing natural cover against ranged attacks but also making navigation slightly more complicated.
