> 👉 **A Garden is a peaceful special room containing trees, herbs, and garden gnomes.**
 
## ℹ️ Description
 
A Garden is a unique GnollHack-specific special room designed as an indoor courtyard or arboretum. It is filled with trees, various herbs, and fungi, providing a natural respite from the dark dungeon. The garden is tended by [[garden gnomes|/Monsters/Gnome]], who can often be found posing as statues.
 
## 🗺️ Generation
 
Gardens can be generated randomly as a special room. They will only generate in rooms that have exactly one door and no stairs. When a garden is generated, the room is cleared of standard dungeon debris and populated with garden-variety trees and a diverse selection of vegetation on the grass.
 
### 👹 Monsters and Statues
 
Unlike standard rooms, gardens generate a very specific set of monsters and statues, which scale depending on the dungeon's difficulty level.
 
There is a 50% chance for a garden to generate with statues acting as traps.
 
| Level Difficulty | Spawning Monsters | Spawning Statues |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low Levels** | [[/Monsters/Hobgoblin]], [[/Monsters/bugbear]] | [[/Monsters/Gnome]] |
| **Mid Levels** | [[/Monsters/Ogre]], [[/Monsters/ogre lord]] | [[/Monsters/Gnome lord]], [[/Monsters/gnome king]], [[/Monsters/gargoyle]] |
| **High Levels** | High-level Ogre-class monsters up to [[/Monsters/ogre overlord]] | [[/Monsters/Winged gargoyle]] |
 
> ℹ️ **Note:** Gnome statues in gardens always spawn wearing a conical hat, blending in as traditional garden gnomes until disturbed.
