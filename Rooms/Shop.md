> 👉 **A Shop is a special room where a shopkeeper sells goods to the player character.**

## ℹ️ Description

A shop is a specialized room filled with items for sale. A shopkeeper stands near the entrance, ensuring you pay for anything you take out of the room. Shops provide a reliable way to purchase much-needed equipment, identify unknown items through price testing, and sell your unwanted loot for gold.

## 🗺️ Generation

Shops are very common special rooms and can be generated randomly in the Dungeons of Doom. The game always tries to generate a shop first before other special rooms. They will only generate in rooms that have exactly one door and no stairs. 

There are many different types of shops, each specializing in different types of items, though some are general stores that sell a bit of everything:
- Armor shops
- Scroll shops
- Potion shops
- Weapon shops
- Food shops
- Ring shops
- Wand shops
- Tool shops
- Book shops
- Candle shops
- [[/Rooms/Reagent Shop|Reagent shops]]
- Health food stores
