> 👉 **A Dwarven Geologist room is a specialized NPC room inhabited by a [[Dwarven Geologist|/Monsters/Dwarven geologist]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Dwarven Geologist room is a GnollHack-specific NPC room. It serves as the study or workspace for a [[Dwarven Geologist|/Monsters/Dwarven geologist]], an NPC who specializes in gems, stones, and earth sciences.

## 🗺️ Generation

Dwarven Geologist rooms are generated as a subtype of the generic NPC room. When an NPC room is generated, there is a chance it will be assigned the Dwarven Geologist subtype, placing a [[Dwarven Geologist|/Monsters/Dwarven geologist]] NPC inside the room.
