> 👉 **A Dwarven Geologist room is a specialized NPC room inhabited by a Dwarven Geologist.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Dwarven Geologist room is a GnollHack-specific NPC room. It serves as the study or workspace for a Dwarven Geologist, an NPC who specializes in gems, stones, and earth sciences.

## 🗺️ Generation

Dwarven Geologist rooms are generated as a subtype of the generic NPC room. When an NPC room is generated, there is a chance it will be assigned the Dwarven Geologist subtype, placing a Dwarven Geologist NPC inside the room.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Gem Identification:** Dwarven Geologists are experts in precious stones. They can offer services to identify the gems and stones you have collected in the dungeon, helping you separate the valuable true gems from worthless glass.
- **Buying and Selling:** They may also buy gems from you or sell specialized mining equipment like pick-axes or touchstones.
- **Interaction:** Use the `#chat` command to interact with the Dwarven Geologist and access their services. Keep your gems organized before speaking to them to streamline the identification process.
