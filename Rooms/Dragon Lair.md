> 👉 **A Dragon Lair is a massive special room filled with dragons, hatchlings, and precious [[gems|/Gems and Stones]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Dragon Lair is a highly dangerous but extremely rewarding GnollHack-specific special room. The room serves as the nesting ground for dragons of a specific color (red, white, blue, green, orange, black, silver, yellow, or gray), containing adult dragons, their hatchlings, or a single ancient dragon. 

## 🗺️ Generation

Dragon Lairs can be generated randomly as a special room on Dungeons of Doom level 17 and deeper, with a 1 in 5 chance of generating.

### 🐉 Monster Assortment

The lair is populated entirely by dragons of a single color type. Depending on room space, it contains either:
- **Ancient Dragon:** A single, extremely powerful Ancient Dragon of that color (1 in 3 chance).
- **Adult & Hatchling Dragons:** 1 to 2 Adult Dragons alongside 1 to 5 Hatchlings of that color (2 in 3 chance).

The dragons generate with a 50% chance of being asleep.

## 💎 Treasure

A Dragon Lair contains a vast amount of gold and [[gems|/Gems and Stones]]. Each dragon spawns on a pile of gold, and they hoard the following treasures:
- **Ancient Dragon:** 3000 to 7000 gold pieces, 5 to 10 [[true gems|/Gems and Stones]] (non-glass), and 1 to 6 other random items.
- **Adult Dragon:** 1000 to 3000 gold pieces, 0 to 5 [[gems|/Gems and Stones]], and 0 to 3 other random items.
- **Dragon Hatchling:** 100 to 300 gold pieces, 0 to 2 [[gems|/Gems and Stones]], and 0 to 1 other random item.

In addition to the hoard, every square in the lair has a 1 in 6 chance of containing gold pieces, alongside a high chance of random [[gems|/Gems and Stones]] and items scattered on the floor. Unlike the [[/Rooms/Garden]], no [[fruit|/Comestibles]] grows in a Dragon Lair.
