> 👉 **A Temple is a special room containing a shrine dedicated to one of the game's deities, tended by a [[priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Temple is a highly valuable special room because it contains a stationary altar (shrine) and an [[/Monsters/aligned priest]]. Temples provide a safe haven for characters of the same alignment and a place to identify the blessed/cursed status (BUC) of items, make donations, or sacrifice monsters.

## 🗺️ Generation

Temples generate on Dungeons of Doom levels between level 9 and Medusa's level. The generation probability depends on whether a temple has already been generated:
- **First Temple:** A 1 in 7 chance per level. If no temple has been generated by the time you reach 5 levels below the Oracle's level, a Temple is guaranteed to generate on that level.
- **Subsequent Temples:** A 1 in 17 chance per level.

The temple contains a central stationary [[altar|/Dungeon/Altars]] dedicated to a random deity (Lawful, Neutral, or Chaotic), tended by a peaceful [[aligned priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]] of the matching alignment.
