> 👉 **A Temple is a specialized NPC room containing a shrine dedicated to one of the game's deities, tended by a peaceful [[aligned priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A **Temple** is a highly valuable, peaceful NPC room that contains a stationary altar (shrine) and a matching **[[/Monsters/Aligned priest]]**. Temples provide a safe haven for characters of the same alignment. They serve as a place to identify the blessed/cursed (BUC) status of items, sacrifice monsters, pray, or obtain various divine services.

## 🗺️ Generation

Temples generate randomly in the [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]] between level 9 and Medusa's level. The generation probability depends on whether a temple has already been generated:
- **First Temple:** A 1 in 7 chance per level. If no temple has been generated by the time you reach 5 levels below the Oracle's level, a Temple is guaranteed to generate on that level.
- **Subsequent Temples:** A 1 in 17 chance per level.

Each temple contains a central stationary altar dedicated to a random deity ([[/lawful]], [[/neutral]], or [[/chaotic]]), tended by a peaceful [[aligned priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]] of the matching alignment.

## 🛠️ Services

Peaceful aligned priests offer several services to characters in exchange for gold pieces:

### ❤️ Healing and Restoration

- **Standard Healing:** Heals the target (either you or a companion/pet) using the [[/Spells/Extra healing]] spell effect.
- **Full Healing:** Fully restores the target's hit points using the [[/Spells/Full healing]] spell effect.
- **Cure Sickness:** Cures the target's sickness using the [[/Spells/Cure sickness]] spell effect.

### ✨ Item Manipulation

- **Bless or Curse an Item:** Blesses or curses a targeted item.

### 🔮 Divination

- **Divination:** Inspects the player's divine status. This service provides information on:
  - Whether you can safely Pray (`Alt-p`).
  - Your deity's anger level.
  - Your prayer timeout status.
  - Your current Luck value.

### 🪄 Teach Spells

Teaches clerical spells to the player if the player does not already know them. The available spells depend on the temple's alignment:

| Temple Alignment | Spells Taught |
|---|---|
| **Common** (All) | [[/Spells/Congregate]], [[/Spells/Minor healing]], [[/Spells/Healing]], [[/Spells/Extra healing]] |
| **[[/Lawful]]** | [[/Spells/Cure blindness]], [[/Spells/Summon celestial dove]] |
| **[[/Neutral]]** | [[/Spells/Stick to snake]], [[/Spells/Create clay golem]] |
| **[[/Chaotic]]** | [[/Spells/Raise minor zombie]], [[/Spells/Replenish undeath]] |
| **Unholy** (Moloch) | [[/Spells/Drain level]], [[/Spells/Summon demon]] |

### 💰 Monetary Contributions

Donate gold to the temple for divine favors (only available inside the temple).

- **Minor Donation:** Bestows a blessing that grants or extends the Clairvoyance intrinsic (by 500 to 999 turns). If coaligned and sinned, increases alignment record by 1.
- **Major Contribution:** May reward devotion by granting or increasing the Protection intrinsic (adds intrinsic AC). If already at maximum protection or capped, it either cleanses sins (restores alignment record to 0 if strayed and it has been at least 5,000 moves since last cleansed) or increases alignment record by 2.
