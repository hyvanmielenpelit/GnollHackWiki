> 👉 **"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a tiny, hidden closet containing an [[orc captain|/Monsters/Orc-captain]] guarding multiple pies.**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It acts as a slightly escalated sequel to the "Orc and the Pie" Easter egg room, increasing both the danger and the reward.

## 🗺️ Generation

This room generates as a tiny 1x1 closet hidden within the dungeon. Inside, you will find:
- An [[orc captain|/Monsters/Orc-captain]]
- Several pies
