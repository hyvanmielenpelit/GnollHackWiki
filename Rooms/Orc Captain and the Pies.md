> 👉 **"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a tiny, hidden closet containing an [[/Monsters/orc-captain]] guarding multiple [[cream pies|/Items/Cream pie]].**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It acts as a slightly escalated sequel to the [[/Rooms/Orc and the Pie]] Easter egg room, increasing both the danger and the reward.

## 🗺️ Generation

This room generates as a tiny 1x1 closet hidden within the dungeon. Inside, you will find:
- An [[orc captain|/Monsters/Orc-captain]] (who is fully geared with orcish equipment and carries one [[cream pie|/Items/Cream pie]])
- A large box containing 5 to 12 [[cream pies|/Items/Cream pie]]
