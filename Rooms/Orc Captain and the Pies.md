> 👉 **"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a tiny, hidden closet containing an [[/Monsters/orc-captain]] guarding multiple [[cream pies|/Items/Cream pie]].**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It acts as a slightly escalated sequel to the [[/Rooms/Orc and the Pie]] Easter egg room, increasing both the danger and the reward.

## 🗺️ Generation

This closet generates as a tiny 1x1 room hidden behind a locked door. It has a 1 in 5 chance of generating on Dungeons of Doom levels 9 to 14, provided it has not been generated yet. Inside, you will find:
- **An Orc Captain:** A single [[orc-captain|/Monsters/Orc-captain]] that has maximum hit points, is fully equipped and wearing [[/Items/orcish chain mail]], [[helm|/Items/Orcish helm]], [[cloak|/Items/Orcish cloak]], [[dagger|/Items/Orcish dagger]], [[shield|/Items/Orcish shield]], [[short sword|/Items/Orcish short sword]], [[short bow|/Items/Orcish short bow]], and carrying 10 to 12 [[arrows|/Items/Orcish arrow]]. He also carries one [[cream pie|/Items/Cream pie]] in his inventory.
- **A Box:** A [[/Items/large box]] containing 5 to 12 [[cream pies|/Items/Cream pie]] and a **blessed [[/Items/potion of gain ability]]**!
