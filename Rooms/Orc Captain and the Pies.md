> 👉 **"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a tiny, hidden closet containing an orc captain guarding multiple pies.**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc Captain and the Pies" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It acts as a slightly escalated sequel to the "Orc and the Pie" Easter egg room, increasing both the danger and the reward.

## 🗺️ Generation

This room generates as a tiny 1x1 closet hidden within the dungeon. Inside, you will find:
- An orc captain
- Several pies

## 🏆 Strategy

- **The Encounter:** Orc captains are significantly more dangerous than standard orcs, boasting better armor, more hit points, and stronger attacks. If you find this closet early in the game, be prepared for a slightly tougher fight than a standard orc.
- **The Reward:** Defeating the orc captain yields multiple pies instead of just one, providing a substantial boost to your food reserves.
