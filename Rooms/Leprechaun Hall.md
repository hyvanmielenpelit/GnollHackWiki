> 👉 **A Leprechaun Hall is a special room completely filled with leprechauns and large amounts of gold.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Leprechaun Hall is a dangerous but potentially rewarding special room. It is filled entirely with [[leprechauns|/Monsters/Leprechaun]] who sit on top of large piles of gold. Because [[leprechauns|/Monsters/Leprechaun]] have a teleportation attack that activates when they hit you or when you hit them, engaging them in melee can quickly scatter them across the level.

## 🗺️ Generation

Leprechaun Halls have a 1 in 8 chance of generating on eligible levels in the Dungeons of Doom, provided that [[leprechauns|/Monsters/Leprechaun]] have not been genocided or made extinct.

When generated, the room will be packed with sleeping [[leprechauns|/Monsters/Leprechaun]], and each [[leprechaun|/Monsters/Leprechaun]] will typically have a significant amount of gold beneath them.
