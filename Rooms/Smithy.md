> 👉 **A Smithy is a special room serving as a blacksmith's workshop.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Smithy is a GnollHack-specific special room that contains a working anvil and a blacksmith NPC. It provides players with a dedicated location to forge, repair, or upgrade their metallic equipment.

## 🗺️ Generation

A Smithy can be generated randomly as a special room. The room typically features an anvil dungeon feature in the center and is tended by a blacksmith.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Forging and Repairing:** The primary draw of a smithy is the anvil. If you have a blacksmith's hammer and the necessary skills, you can use the anvil to forge new items, repair damaged weapons and armor, or improve your current gear.
- **The Blacksmith:** The blacksmith NPC may offer services or sell specialized metallic gear, depending on their level and disposition.
- **Safe Workspace:** Because the smithy is a dedicated room, it provides a relatively safe, enclosed environment to perform time-consuming crafting actions without being immediately swarmed by wandering monsters, provided you close the door.
