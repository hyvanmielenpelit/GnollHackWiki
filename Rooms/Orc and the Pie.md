> 👉 **"Orc and the Pie" is a tiny, hidden closet containing a single [[orc|/Monsters/Orc]] guarding a pie.**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc and the Pie" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It is a humorous Easter egg referencing the famous micro-adventure "The Orc and the Pie," which is widely considered the shortest and simplest Dungeons & Dragons adventure ever written.

## 🗺️ Generation

This room generates as a tiny 1x1 closet hidden within the dungeon. True to its namesake, it contains exactly two things:
- An [[orc|/Monsters/Orc]]
- A pie
