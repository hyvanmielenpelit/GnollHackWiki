> 👉 **"Orc and the Pie" is a tiny, hidden closet containing a single [[orc|/Monsters/Orc]] guarding a [[cream pie|/Items/Cream pie]].**

## ℹ️ Description

"Orc and the Pie" is a GnollHack-specific special closet. It is a humorous Easter egg referencing the famous micro-adventure "The Orc and the Pie," which is widely considered the shortest and simplest Dungeons & Dragons adventure ever written. Its sequel room is [[/Rooms/Orc Captain and the Pies]].

## 🗺️ Generation

This closet generates as a tiny 1x1 room hidden behind a locked door. It has a 1 in 3 chance of generating on Dungeons of Doom levels 3 to 6, provided it has not been generated yet. True to its namesake, it contains:
- **An Orc:** A single [[orc|/Monsters/Orc]] that has maximum hit points, is generated in an asleep/waiting state, and is fully equipped and wearing [[orcish chain mail|/Items/Orcish chain mail]], an [[orcish helm|/Items/Orcish helm]], an [[orcish cloak|/Items/Orcish cloak]], an [[orcish dagger|/Items/Orcish dagger]], an [[orcish shield|/Items/Orcish shield]], and an [[orcish short sword|/Items/Orcish short sword]].
- **A Box:** A [[large box|/Items/Large box]] containing exactly one [[cream pie|/Items/Cream pie]].
