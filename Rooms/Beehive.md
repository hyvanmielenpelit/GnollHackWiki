> 👉 **A Beehive is a special room filled with killer bees and large quantities of royal jelly.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Beehive is a highly specialized room containing only killer bees, the occasional queen bee, and multiple lumps of royal jelly on the floor. Beehives are notoriously dangerous for early-game characters due to the bees' speed, swarming behavior, and poisonous stingers.

## 🗺️ Generation

Beehives have a 1 in 5 chance of generating on eligible levels starting from dungeon level 10, provided that killer bees have not been genocided or made extinct.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Poison Resistance:** It is highly recommended to have poison resistance before engaging a beehive. Killer bees have a poisonous sting that can quickly kill a character who lacks resistance.
- **Choke Points:** Stand in the doorway to fight the bees one at a time. Their high speed means they will quickly surround you if you step inside the room.
- **Royal Jelly:** The primary reward for clearing a beehive is royal jelly. Royal jelly is a highly nutritious food that increases your strength, cures wounded legs, and provides a significant boost to your health points when eaten.
