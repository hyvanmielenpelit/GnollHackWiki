> 👉 **An Engineering Bay is a specialized NPC room found exclusively on the Modron Plane, inhabited by the Warp Engineer.**

## ℹ️ Description

An **Engineering Bay** is the high-tech workshop of the **Warp Engineer**. The Warp Engineer is a peaceful [[/Monsters/Modron Quarton]] NPC who is an expert in portal technology and planar engineering. The room features high-tech aesthetics, custom Modron-themed tiles, and the Warp Engineer who offers extremely powerful crafting and travel services.

## 🗺️ Generation

The Engineering Bay spawns at a fixed location on the [[/Dungeon/Plane of the Modron]] (at coordinates 40,6). The room is lit and plays the unique Warp Engineer background music. Distant sounds of electrical hums, discharges, and levitation can be heard when approaching.

## 🛠️ Services

The Warp Engineer offers the following advanced services to adventurers for gold (with costs adjusted by Charisma) and specific components:

- **Forge a Pair of Artificial Wings:** Craft wings that grant permanent flight. Requires **12 [[feathers|/Items/Feather]]** and gold (`500 + 50 per character level`).
- **Forge a Cubic Gate:** Craft a [[/Items/cubic gate]] tool, allowing portal travel between branches. Requires **1 [[/Items/dilithium crystal]]** and gold (`25,000 + 2,500 per character level`).
- **Open a Branch Portal:** Open a portal leading to another major dungeon branch.
- **Recharge Items:** Recharge [[/wands]] and other magic items.
- **Fully Recharge Items:** Fully (blessed) recharge [[/wands]] and magic items.
- **Identify Gems, Stones, and Charged Items:** Identify [[/amulets]], [[/rings]], [[/wands]], [[gems|/Gems and Stones]], and [[stones|/Gems and Stones]] in your inventory.
- **Commerce:** Buy [[dilithium crystals|/Items/Dilithium crystal]] and other [[gems|/Gems and Stones]] from you.
