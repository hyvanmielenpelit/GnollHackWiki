> 👉 **A Swamp is a damp special room characterized by multiple pools of water and amphibious monsters.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Swamp is a water-logged special room. The floor of the room is converted entirely to swampy grass, and a checkerboard pattern of water pools covers half of the squares. It is inhabited by aquatic pool monsters and moldy land fungi, and is a rich source of wild berries and mushrooms.

## 🗺️ Generation

Swamps have a 1 in 6 chance of generating on Dungeons of Doom levels starting from level 14. When generated on a level, the game will attempt to convert up to 5 ordinary rooms into swamps.

## 👾 Monsters

- **Pool Squares:** The water pools are populated with aquatic monsters, primarily [[giant eels|/Monsters/Giant eel]] (80%), [[piranhas|/Monsters/Piranha]] (10%), or [[electric eels|/Monsters/Electric eel]] (10%).
- **Land Squares:** The dry grass squares have a 25% chance of spawning sleeping fungi or mold monsters (like a [[violet fungus|/Monsters/Violet fungus]] or [[brown mold|/Monsters/Brown mold]]). Standard terrestrial monsters (like snakes or crocodiles) do not generate as part of the room itself.

## 🍇 Vegetation and Foraging

The swampy grass tiles are fertile ground for various edible and dangerous plants. Each dry square has separate chances to contain:
- **Mushrooms (14% chance):** [[Chanterelle|/Items/Chanterelle]], [[penny bun|/Items/Penny bun]], [[champignon|/Items/Champignon]], or [[fly agaric|/Items/Fly agaric]].
- **Dragon Fruit (12.5% chance):** A valuable [[dragon fruit|/Items/Dragon fruit]].
- **Cloudberry (10% chance):** A [[cloudberry|/Items/Cloudberry]].
- **Poisonous Fungi (10% chance):** [[Death cap|/Items/Death cap]] or [[panther cap|/Items/Panther cap]].
- **Phantomberry (2% chance):** A rare and magical [[phantomberry|/Items/Phantomberry]].
