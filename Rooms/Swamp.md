> 👉 **A Swamp is a damp special room characterized by multiple pools of water and amphibious monsters.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Swamp is a hazardous room filled with pools of water, making navigation difficult and dangerous. It is populated by aquatic and amphibious monsters, such as [[snakes|/Monsters/Snake]], [[crocodiles|/Monsters/Crocodile]], [[giant eels|/Monsters/Giant eel]], and various fungi.

## 🗺️ Generation

Swamps have a 1 in 6 chance of generating on eligible levels starting from dungeon level 16. The room's floor is heavily dotted with pools of water. Swamps may also randomly contain [[dragon fruits|/Items/Dragon fruit]].
