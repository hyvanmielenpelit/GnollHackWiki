> 👉 **A Graveyard, also known as a Morgue, is a special room filled with undead creatures and graves.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Graveyard is a chilling special room that contains a large number of undead monsters, such as [[ghosts|/Monsters/Ghost]], [[zombies|/Monsters/Zombies, skeletons, and other lesser undead]], [[mummies|/Monsters/Mummies and other greater undead]], and [[vampires|/Monsters/Vampire]]. The floor is typically dotted with graves and sometimes contains chests with loot. It poses a significant threat due to the life-draining attacks common among higher-level undead.

## 🗺️ Generation

Graveyards have a 1 in 6 chance of generating on eligible levels starting from dungeon level 12. They are always generated unlit, regardless of the level's overall lighting.
