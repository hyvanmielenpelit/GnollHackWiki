> 👉 **A Throne Room is a special room that contains a throne and a large number of monsters guarding it.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Throne Room is a special room easily identified by the presence of a throne in the center and a multitude of sleeping monsters surrounding it. The room usually contains a chest with loot, making it a lucrative but dangerous area for early game characters.

## 🗺️ Generation

Throne rooms can be generated randomly in the Dungeons of Doom. They have a 1 in 6 chance of generating on levels eligible for special rooms. The room is always lit and the floor is completely filled with monsters that are generated asleep, provided you do not make too much noise.

The monsters in a throne room are often led by a prominent monster acting as the ruler (like an Orc king or an Ogre king), accompanied by their retinue. 

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Stealth and Noise:** Avoid waking up the entire room at once. If you do not have Stealth, try to take out monsters one by one at the entrance. Waking them all up can quickly overwhelm a low-level character.
- **Loot:** The chest inside a throne room often contains gold and valuable items.
- **The Throne:** Sitting on the throne has various random magical effects, which can range from identifying items and granting wishes, to cursing your items or causing a trap door to open under you. It is highly recommended to wait until you have high Luck before sitting on a throne.
