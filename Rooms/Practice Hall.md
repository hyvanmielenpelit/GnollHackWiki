> 👉 **A Practice Hall is a specialized NPC room where you can find Lindisse, the Elven Bard.**

## ℹ️ Description

A Practice Hall is a GnollHack-specific NPC room that serves as a musical training ground or performance area. It is uniquely inhabited by Lindisse, a named Elven Bard NPC.

## 🗺️ Generation

Practice Halls are generated as a subtype of the generic NPC room. When an NPC room is generated, there is a chance it will be assigned the Elven Bard subtype, placing Lindisse the Elven Bard inside the room. The room usually has a fireplace.

## 🏆 Strategy

- **Lindisse:** Lindisse is a unique Elven Bard NPC. By interacting with her using the `#chat` command, players can access her services. 
- **Services:** Bards typically offer services related to songs and rumors. Lindisse may sing songs that can have various effects or provide the player with useful (or misleading) rumors about the dungeon.
- **Musical Instruments:** Since the room is a practice hall, it is an appropriate place to play musical instruments if you have them, though the primary attraction remains interacting with Lindisse herself.
