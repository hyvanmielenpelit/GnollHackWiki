> 👉 **A Practice Hall is a specialized NPC room where you can find Lindisse, the [[Elven Bard|/Monsters/Elven bard]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Practice Hall is a GnollHack-specific NPC room that serves as a musical training ground or performance area. It is uniquely inhabited by Lindisse, a named [[Elven Bard|/Monsters/Elven bard]] NPC.

## 🗺️ Generation

Practice Halls are generated as a subtype of the generic NPC room. When an NPC room is generated, there is a chance it will be assigned the Elven Bard subtype, placing Lindisse the [[Elven Bard|/Monsters/Elven bard]] inside the room. The room usually has a fireplace.
