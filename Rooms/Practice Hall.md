> 👉 **A Practice Hall is a specialized NPC room where you can find Lindisse, the [[/Monsters/Elven Bard]].**

## ℹ️ Description

A Practice Hall is a GnollHack-specific NPC room that serves as a performance room. It is uniquely inhabited by Lindisse, a named, peaceful [[elven bard|/Monsters/Elven Bard]] NPC. The room features parquet-style floors, a fireplace, and always contains a decorative painting on the wall.

## 🗺️ Generation

Practice Halls are not part of the standard random NPC room pool. Instead, a Practice Hall is generated on a random level of the Dungeons of Doom between dungeon level 2 and Medusa's level, exactly once per game. 

## 🛠️ Services

Lindisse, the Elven Bard, is friendly and offers the following unique interactions:
- **Sing Songs:** She sings old elven songs to aid adventurers. This plays custom music ("A Elbereth Gilthoniel").
- **Special Hints:** She provides players with helpful rumors and tips.
- **True Rumors:** She has a high chance of giving true, useful advice.
