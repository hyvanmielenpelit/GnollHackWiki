## Overview

Dexterity:

- Increases the chance to hit with melee and ranged weapons
- Decreases your [[Armor Class (AC)|Armor Class (AC) and Magic Cancellation (MC)]]
- Increases your chance to hit monsters with thrown potions.

Lower AC is better.
