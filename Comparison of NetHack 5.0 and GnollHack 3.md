# NetHack 5.0 vs. GnollHack 3: Which One Should You Play?

> 👉 **A quick comparison between mainline NetHack 5.0 (the traditional hardcore classic) and GnollHack 3 (the modern, visual variant) to help you choose the right style of dungeon crawl.**

## 🧬 How They Are Related

* **Mainline vs. Fork:** **NetHack 5.0** is the direct continuation of the original 1987 classic. **GnollHack 3** is a major "fork" (variant) that split off from the older NetHack 3.6.2 codebase.
* **Shared Roots:** Both games share the same fundamental dungeon structures and basic monster classes.
* **The Divergence:** NetHack 5.0 refines the hardcore, classic keyboard-driven experience, while GnollHack 3 rebuilds the engine to support modern graphics, music, sounds, voiceovers, touchscreen-friendly controls, and mobile operating system support.

## 🎯 Meta-Level Comparison

| Aspect | NetHack 5.0 | GnollHack 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Philosophy** | An uncompromising fantasy simulation that prioritizes mechanical depth, player discovery, and high-stakes survival. | A modern reimagining that lowers the barrier to entry by blending classic roguelike systems with contemporary RPG design and rich presentation. |
| **Target Audience** | Purists, veterans, and fans of classic ASCII/keyboard-driven gaming. | Beginners, mobile gamers, and players seeking a fresh, audiovisual tactical experience. |
| **Key Selling Points** | <ul><li>**The Ultimate Classic:** The definitive version of the legendary 1987 roguelike.</li><li>**Brutal Challenge:** Unforgiving permadeath and complex mechanics.</li><li>**Classic Feel, New Polish:** Flawless modern stability and long-requested features like a built-in tutorial.</li></ul> | <ul><li>**Audiovisual Overhaul:** Real 2D animations, voiceovers, and a full musical score.</li><li>**ARPG Loot System:** Mythic affixes, Elite weapon tiers, and 22 gear slots.</li><li>**Accessibility:** 7 difficulty levels, optional permadeath, and streamlined UI.</li></ul> |
| **Key Pain Points** | <ul><li>**Archaic UI:** Requires memorizing dozens of obscure keyboard commands.</li><li>**Spoiler Dependency:** Exact stats and mechanics are hidden; playing without external wikis is notoriously difficult.</li><li>**Instadeaths & Tedium:** Features "unfair" instant deaths and repetitive, long mazes in the late game.</li></ul> | <ul><li>**Inventory Management:** The massive amount of loot, 22 gear slots, and magic reagents can cause inventory fatigue.</li><li>**System Complexity:** The deep mechanics can still be overwhelming to learn.</li><li>**Grid-Based Pacing:** Despite modern graphics, it remains a methodical, turn-based grid game which may feel slow to action-roguelite fans.</li></ul> |
| **Why Play in 2026?** | To play a historically significant masterpiece, modernized to run flawlessly today while preserving its hardcore 1980s ethos. | To enjoy the depth of a traditional roguelike wrapped in the presentation and quality-of-life standards of modern gaming. |

> ℹ️ **Versioning Note:** GnollHack uses a dual versioning scheme. The modern port version '3.x' translates directly to the legacy library version '4.3.0 Build x'. For example, modern port version 3.1 corresponds to legacy version 4.3.0 Build 1.

## ⚖️ High-Level Comparison

| Feature | NetHack 5.0 | GnollHack 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Difficulty Options** | 📈 Single, steep difficulty curve. Permadeath is mandatory. | 🌟 7 difficulty levels. Includes "Casual" and "Modern" modes where permadeath is optional. |
| **Graphics & UI** | 🖥️ ASCII graphics or simple static 2D tiles. Traditional terminal UI. | ✨ Modern UI optimized for Desktop/Mobile. High-res animated graphics (23,000+ base tiles). |
| **Audio** | ❌ No sound. | 🔊 Full soundtrack (14 original music tracks and 23 other music tracks), sound effects, and 5,000+ voiceovers. |
| **Controls** | ⌨️ Dozens of keyboard commands must be memorized (e.g., 'q' to quaff, 'r' to read). | 🖱️ Point-and-click context menus. |
| **Information Accessibility**| ❓ Relies heavily on external wikis/spoilers. Exact damage values and stats are hidden. | 🔍 Examine (`Alt+x`) command shows exact item stats, magical effects, and damage formulas in-game. |
| **Pacing** | 🐢 Slow and methodical. Gehennom contains long, repetitive mazes. | ⚡ Faster leveling (level cap 50). Gehennom mazes have been completely removed (except special levels). |

## ⚔️ Gameplay & Mechanics

| System | NetHack 5.0 | GnollHack 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Economy** | ❌ Gold has very limited uses. | 💰 Gold is highly valuable. Used to pay NPCs to identify items, heal, or purchase services. |
| **Item Loot & Gear** | 🗡️ Standard item list. Basic enchantments (+1, +2, etc.). | 💎 ARPG-style loot. Weapons spawn with quality tiers (Exceptional, Elite, Celestial) and Mythic/Legendary affixes. |
| **Equipment Slots** | 👕 Traditional slots (Armor, Cloak, Shield, Helm, Gloves, Boots). | 🧥 22 slots. Adds dedicated slots for Robes (worn over armor), Bracers, and 5 Miscellaneous items. |
| **Magic System** | 🧠 Memorization (spells are forgotten over time). | 🔮 Reagent-based. Over 200 spells prepared by mixing ingredients; features armor penalties and cooldowns. |
| **Ranged Combat** | 🐢 Weapon swapping costs a turn. Firing at melee range is allowed without penalty. | 🏹 Instant weapon swapping (0 turns). Buffed ranged combat, but firing at adjacent enemies incurs a severe penalty. |
| **Companions / Pets** | 🐾 Simple pet pathing; pets frequently get stuck or lost. | 🧠 Advanced pathfinding. Pets can be commanded to wear/drop gear and hold position. Hireable mercenaries added. |

## 🧙‍♂️ Details for NetHack Veterans

If you already have a few ascensions under your belt in NetHack, NetHack 5.0 will feel like coming home. However, GnollHack 3 introduces major mechanical shifts that will challenge your muscle memory:

| System / Mechanic | Traditional NetHack (5.0) | GnollHack 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Touch of Death** | 🛡️ Magic Resistance fully protects you from the Touch of Death. | 💀 Magic Resistance **no longer protects** you. You specifically need dedicated Death Resistance gear (e.g., protection from undeath). |
| **Magic Cancellation (MC)** | 🧥 Determined by a single item (typically your cloak). | 🧺 Stacks cumulatively across **all equipped gear** to act as a saving throw against petrification/status effects. |
| **Instant Deaths** | ⏳ Deadly events like drowning or strangling can end your run instantly or in a single turn. | ⏱️ **Usually Delayed.** You get several turns of grace to react and save yourself. |
| **Boss Covetousness** | 🏃‍♂️ Bosses constantly teleport away to heal and may steal your quest artifact. | ⚔️ Frustrating teleportation mechanic is **removed from almost all monsters**. |
| **Attribute Gains (Stats)** | 🏋️ Attributes increase slowly and semi-randomly through hidden "exercise" actions. | 🍎 Attributes increase **instantly** upon eating specific corpses or fruits. The exercise mechanic has been removed. |
| **Playable Races** | 🧙‍♂️ Gnomes are a playable race. | 🧌 Gnomes are replaced entirely by the playable **Gnoll** race, featuring unique stats, traits, and gear. |

## 🏁 Conclusion

**Play NetHack 5.0 if:** You want the quintessential, brutal, classic ASCII roguelike experience. It is the definitive update to the traditional formula, perfect for purists who enjoy meticulous resource management and a steep, unforgiving learning curve.

**Play GnollHack 3 if:** You want a deeply tactical, modern RPG experience with beautiful graphics, music, and voice acting. It respects your time by removing tedious mazes while adding massive replayability through ARPG-style loot, 7 difficulty levels, and a vast new spell system.
