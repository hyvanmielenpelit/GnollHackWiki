## Level 4 divination spell

- **Attributes:** Higher of intelligence, wisdom, and charisma
- **Mana cost:** 36.0
- **Casting time:** 1 round
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Targeting:** None
- **Effect type:** True seeing
- **Duration:** 10d10+600 rounds
- **Train chance:** 100%
- **Base write cost:** 60 charges
- **Write cost:** From half to full base cost
- **Components:** Verbal, Material

## Material components - 8 castings

1. a pearl

## Description

The caster becomes able to see through invisibility, illusions, mimicry, and hallucinations.
