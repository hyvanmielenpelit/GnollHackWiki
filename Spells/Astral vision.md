## Level 10 divination spell

- **Attributes:** Higher of intelligence, wisdom, and charisma
- **Mana cost:** 320
- **Casting time:** 1 round
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Targeting:** None
- **Effect type:** Astral vision
- **Duration:** 10d10+600 rounds
- **Train chance:** 100%
- **Base write cost:** 120 charges
- **Write cost:** From half to full base cost
- **Components:** Verbal, Material

## Material components - 3 castings

1. a ruby

## Description

The caster becomes able to see through walls within a radius of 5 squares
