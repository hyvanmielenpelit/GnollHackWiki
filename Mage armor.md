- **Attributes:** Higher of intelligence and charisma
- **Mana cost:** 12.0
- **Casting time:** 1 round
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Targeting:** None
- **Duration:** 4d20+500 rounds

- **Write cost:** From half to full base cost
