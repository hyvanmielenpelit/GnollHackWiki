## GnollHack Animations with Bella

![Bella and Sir Max](/uploads/GnollHack%20Animations/Bella-and-Sir-Max-h512.png)

**Bella**, the gnoll adventuress, and her dog Welsh springer spaniel **Sir Max** appear in the following short-form animations:

- [GnollHack Animations with Bella Playlist on YouTube Shorts](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLns1Y9tnPp6Eh5jrd_3nMdFNUw_hs-7Mb)

While many Bella animations take place in the Dungeons of Doom, several of them are set in modern, non-adventuring settings.

### Bella's Devices

In some animations, Bella can be seen using a tablet — called iPaw — or a smartphone — called iPwn — manufactured by Paw Technologies Inc., a multinational technology conglomerate owned by a group of wealthy gnolls.
