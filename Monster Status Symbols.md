# Monster Status Symbols

In GnollHack, monsters may have small status symbols attached to their sprites in the game UI. These icons provide important information about the monster's current state, level, hunger, or relationship to the player.

## Generic Monster Status Symbols

The following status symbols can appear on any eligible monster (hostile, peaceful, or tame).

| Name | Icon | Meaning |
| :--- | :--: | :--- |
| **Bounty** | ![bounty](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_bounty.webp) | The monster is the target of a bounty quest (active for Knights). |
| **Busy** | ![busy](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status2_frozen.webp) | The monster is busy doing something, preventing it from moving or acting. |
| **Detected** | ![detectmark](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_detectmark.webp) | The monster has been detected (e.g., via telepathy, warning, or detect monsters) but might not be in your direct line of sight. |
| **Eating** | ![eating](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status2_eating.webp) | The monster is busy eating something on the ground. |
| **High-Level** | ![high-level](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status2_high-level.webp) | The monster is at least 5 levels higher than its base level, making it significantly stronger and more dangerous than normally. |
| **Grabber** | ![grabber](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_ustuck.webp) | The monster is grabbing you. |
| **Peaceful** | ![peacemark](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_peacemark.webp) | The monster is peaceful and will not attack you unless provoked. |
| **Saddled** | ![saddled](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_saddled.webp) | The monster is equipped with a saddle and can be ridden as a steed. |
| **Town Guard (Hostile)** | ![townguard-hostile](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_townguard-hostile.webp) | The monster is a hostile town guard. |
| **Town Guard (Peaceful)** | ![townguard-peaceful](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_townguard-peaceful.webp) | The monster is a peaceful town guard. |
| **Trapped** | ![trapped](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_trapped.webp) | The monster is currently stuck in a trap (such as a pit, web, or bear trap). |

## Pet-Only Status Symbols

The following status symbols are exclusive to your tame pets and help you monitor their health, hunger, and status.

| Name | Icon | Meaning |
| :--- | :--: | :--- |
| **Tame (Pet)** | ![petmark](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_petmark.webp) | The monster is tame and acts as your loyal pet. |
| **Cooldown** | ![cooldown](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_spec-used.webp) | The monster has used its special ability, and it is on cooldown. |
| **Critical HP** | ![critical-hp](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_critical-hp.webp) | The pet's health is critically low (at or below 25% of its maximum HP). |
| **Hungry** | ![hungry](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_hungry.webp) | The pet is hungry and needs to be fed. |
| **Inventory** | ![inventory](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_inventory.webp) | The pet is carrying items in its inventory. |
| **Low HP** | ![low-hp](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_low-hp.webp) | The pet's health is low (at or below 50% but above 25% of its maximum HP). |
| **Weak** | ![weak](/uploads/Monster%20Status%20Symbols/status_weak.webp) | The pet is weak from severe hunger and may collapse or suffer penalties if not fed immediately. |
