## Accessing Equipment Screen from Inventory

In the top left corner of the **inventory screen**, there is a **flip button**:

![Flip button in the inventory screen](/uploads/Equipment%20Screen/flip-button.webp)

When you click it, the inventory screen will flip to an **equipment screen**.

## Equipment Screen

![Equipment screen](/uploads/Equipment%20Screen/equipment-screen.webp)

The equipment screen will give you a quick look at what equipment slots there are and which one of them have been equipped with an item.

*Clicking an item icon* in the equipment screen will show its **item row** below the equipment slots.

*Double-clicking an item icon* in the equipment screen will open an item action menu.
