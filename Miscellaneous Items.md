## Miscellaneous Item Slots

You can wear up to *5 miscellaneous items*, but only 1 per miscellaneous magic item slot. The slots are:
- Belt
- Brooch
- Earrings
- Eyeglasses
- Headband
- Ioun stone
- Mask
- Nose ring
- Watch
- Wings
