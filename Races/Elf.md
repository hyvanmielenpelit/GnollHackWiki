## Description

Elves are highly intelligent but physically fragile race. This makes them excellent spell casters.

## Attribute Scores

| Attribute Score | Modifier | Maximum |
| :-------------- | :------: | :-----: |
| Strength | -2 | 18 |
| Dexterity | +0 | 18 |
| Constitution | -2 | 16 |
| Intelligence | +2 | 20 |
| Wisdom | +2 | 20 |
| Charisma | +0 | 18 |

| Hit points | 10 | +1 |
| Mana | 1d12 (avg. 6.5) | +2 |

## Racial Traits

- Increased nutrition from elven waybread
- Infravision
- Elves may be peaceful to you

- Sleep resistance
