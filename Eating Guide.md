## Dangerous Food

### Food That Causes Monster Aggravation Intrinsic 

Eating certain monsters causes the player character to receive the **Monster Aggravation** intrinsic under two main categories: **domestic animals** and **cannibalism**. 

These rules apply to eating the monster's **corpse**, eating a **tin** of that monster, or eating the monster's **brain** (while polymorphed into a Mind Flayer/tentacled monster).

However, if your character is a [[/Roles/Caveman]] or is of the [[/Races/Orc]] race, they are permitted to commit cannibalism and eat domestic animals without penalty.

For all other character roles and races, eating the following monsters triggers the Monster Aggravation intrinsic:

### 1. Domestic Animals

Eating any of the domestic pets below:

* Little Dog
* Dog
* Large Dog
* Kitten
* Housecat
* Large Cat

### 2. Cannibalism

* **Starting Species:** Monsters belonging to the same race as your starting race (e.g. Human, Elf, Dwarf, Gnome, Gnoll).
* **Polymorphed Species:** Monsters belonging to the same race as your current polymorphed form.
* **Lycanthropic Beast:** If you are afflicted with lycanthropy, the corresponding were-beast or its animal counterpart (e.g. wolf, rat, jackal).
