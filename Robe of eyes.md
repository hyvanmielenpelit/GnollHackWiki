## Armor: Robe

- **Base value:** 400 gold
- **Base material:** Cloth
- **Armor quality:** Cannot have quality

- **Enchantable:** Yes
- **Safe enchantable level:** +3 or below
- **Mythic status:** Cannot be mythic

1. Infravision when worn
2. See invisible when worn
3. Searching when worn
