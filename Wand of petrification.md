## Wand
- **Weight:** 0.4 lbs
- **Base value:** 500 gold
- **Base material:** Iron
- **Skill:** Wand
- **Wand effect range:** 40'
- **Charges:** Yes
- **Maximum charges:** 5
- **Recharging type:** Rechargeable normally as wand

## Description

Shoots a petrification ray that causes monsters on its way to be turned to stone.
