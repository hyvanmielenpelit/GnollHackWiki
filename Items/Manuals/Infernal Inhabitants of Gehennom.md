## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Demons and devils are immune to death attacks.
2. Some devils can spawn with a bullwhip that can disarm you from your weapon. It is best to kill them from a distance.
3. Demons and devils can gate in more of their kind with their melee attack. It is often best to kill them from a distance.
4. The topmost level of Gehennom is the Valley of the Dead, which is inhabited by a large number of undead beings.
5. You will need at least drain resistance to successfully fight these creatures.
6. The entrance to lower levels of Gehennom is guarded by the dreaded dracolich, which can breathe a death ray.
7. Be sure to possess either reflection or death resistance before engaging with the dracolich.
