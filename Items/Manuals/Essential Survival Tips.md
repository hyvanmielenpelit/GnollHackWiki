## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You can cure a disease, such as mummy rot or food poisoning, by eating a fig.
2. Also, a priest can cure your disease for a fee. Furthermore, praying can cure a disease as well.
3. You can cure lycanthropy by drinking a potion of holy water or by eating a sprig of wolfsbane.
4. Blessed potions of healing increase your maximum hit points if you drink them at full health.
5. It is a good practice to drink many blessed potions of healing early on to maximise your hit points.
6. You cannot swim. Do not try to enter deep water, such as moat, or you may drown.
7. Monsters cannot engulf and digest you, if you are wearing a ring of slow digestion.
8. Tentacled ones cannot eat your brain, if you are wearing a ring of sustain ability. They also cannot cause amnesia.
