- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You should acquire various resistances to better withstand the challenges ahead.
2. Most critical resistances are magic resistance, reflection, and paralysis resistance.
3. Also poison, sleep, sickness, petrification, and death resistances are important for survival.
4. Certain magic items may bestow you with some of these resistances.
5. Your role and race can also confer you resistances as you advance in experience levels.
6. A suitable cloak, robe, or ioun stone will confer you magic resistance.
7. You can obtain reflection from an appropriate amulet, pair of bracers, or shield.
8. Paralysis resistance is conferred only by a ring or a mythic weapon or armor of a suitable type.
9. Get poison resistance early on. Look for a ring or an amulet, or eat a suitable monster corpse.
