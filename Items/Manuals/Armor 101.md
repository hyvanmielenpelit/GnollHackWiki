## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Armor gives Armor Class (AC), which reduces the chance of a monster in hitting you. The lower the AC the better the armor.
2. Total AC below 0 also reduces damage.
3. Armor also gives Magic Cancellation (MC), which reduces the success chance of touch-based special attacks. The higher the MC the better the protection.
4. Robe is a special armor slot that does not stack with body armor. The game uses the superior AC and MC of the body armor and the robe.
5. Armor may also give you spell casting penalty, which reduces the chance of casting somatic spells. It does not affect non-somatic spells.
6. A crystal plate mail gives you reflection.
