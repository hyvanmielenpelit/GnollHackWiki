## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You can stand on a scroll of scare monster and monsters cannot attack you.
2. A cursed scroll of scare monster will turn to dust if picked up.
3. A scroll of scare monster will lose one level of the blessed/uncursed/cursed status when dropped on the ground.
4. If you attack while standing on a scroll of scare monster, the scroll will become cursed.
5. It is good practice to bless scrolls of scare monster, so that you can drop them on the ground and pick up again.
