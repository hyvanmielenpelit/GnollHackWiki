## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Your encumbrance limit is based on your strength and constitution.
2. If you are burdened, your speed will drop by 25%.
3. Your speed determines how fast you can move and attack.
4. Skills increase your chance of hitting with an armor or casting a spell. You receive a skill slot for each level above 1.
5. Skills typically require 1/2/3/4/5 skill slots to increase them to the next level at Unskilled/Basic/Skilled/Expert/Master level.
6. You can reassign your skill points if you read a scroll of retraining.
7. High strength gives you bonus to hit and to damage and more carrying capacity.
8. High dexterity gives you bonus to hit and to AC.
9. High consitution gives you bonus hit points and more carrying capacity.
10. High intelligence gives you more mana and helps you to cast some spells better.
11. High wisdom gives you more mana and helps you to cast some spells better.
12. High charisma allows you to get discount on sold items and services. It also helps you to cast some spells better.
