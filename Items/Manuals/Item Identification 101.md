## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You need to identify items before you know what they do.
2. The items can be blessed, uncursed, or cursed. Uncursed items are normal.
3. Blessed items usually grant an additional bonus.
4. Cursed items have adverse effects and you cannot remove them once equipped.
5. Equipment can also have positive or negative enchantment bonuses.
6. Equipment with positive enchantment bonuses work better, while those with negative enchantment work worse.
7. A scroll of identify identifies 1-3 items; 3 if the scroll is blessed, 2 if uncursed, and 1 if cursed.
8. You can drop items on an altar to identify their blessed/uncursed/cursed status.
9. Some pets do not step on cursed items.
10. You can talk to shopkeepers and the Oracle to identify items for a fee.
11. You can talk to many NPCs, such as artificers, to identify some item classes for a fee.
