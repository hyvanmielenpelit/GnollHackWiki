## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Starting pets do not step voluntarily on cursed items.
2. This is a great way to identify whether an item is cursed or not.
3. If a pet steps relucatantly on an item, that item is cursed.
4. You can yell for your pet, if it is far away, and it will come to you.
5. When you go up or down the stairs, your pet needs to be at your side or at most two squares away for it to join you.
6. You can chat to your pet to give it commands or to feed it with food.
7. You can hire some peaceful monsters to join your party for a fee.
8. You can equip your hirelings with items by chatting to them.
