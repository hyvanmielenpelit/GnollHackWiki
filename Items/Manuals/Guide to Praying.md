## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You may pray on turn 301 earliest.
2. Praying at low hit points or in a severe condition will heal you, if your God is not angry with you.
3. After successfully praying, it is good practice to wait 1000 turns before praying again.
4. If you pray too often, your God gets angry and your adventure is ruined.
5. A blessed prayerstone will shimmer when it is safe to pray.
6. You can make a prayerstone blessed by sacrificing on the altar while the stone is in open inventory.
7. Praying does not work in Gehennom.
