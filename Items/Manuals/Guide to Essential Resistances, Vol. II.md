## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You should acquire various resistances to better withstand the challenges ahead.
2. Some resistances such as sleep, sickness, and petrification resistances can be somewhat hard to come by.
3. It is important to get sleep resistance early on. Look for an elf or gelatinous cube corpse.
4. A healer's beak mask can confer you sickness resistance.
5. An amulet versus petrification will grant you petrification resistance.
6. Look for an appropriate amulet, a ring, or a gown to get death resistance.
7. There are also resistances against brain damaging and blinding attacks.
8. A nose ring of cerebral safeguarding will protect your brain.
9. Certain eyeglasses and goggles can confer you protection against blinding attacks.
