## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. It is good practice to genocide dangerous monsters with a blessed scroll of genocide. This will prevent them appearing later in the game.
2. You can create multiple monsters of your choice with a cursed scroll of genocide.
3. Wizard of Yendor may replace your amulet with a cheap plastic imitation. It is good practice to name the real amulet as soon as you get it.
4. It is essential for survival that you get good protective equipment.
5. You need at least magic resistance, reflection, paralysis resistance, sleep resistance, and death resistance to survive some nasty attacks.
6. Magic resistance will protect you from polymorph traps and some spell caster spells.
7. Reflection will protect you from rays.
8. Paralysis resistance will protect you from thrown potion of paralysis and paralysing monsters, such as gelatinous cube.
9. Sleep resistance will protect you from sleep gas traps and thrown potions of sleep.
10. Death resistance will protect you from some instant death attacks, such as the touch of death spell of a greater mummy high priest.
