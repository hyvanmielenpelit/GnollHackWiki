## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Do not eat too much, or you may choke to death.
2. Corpses rot very fast. Do not eat rotten corpses or you will be food poisoned and die.
3. Gnolls can smell which corpses are safe to eat and which are not.
4. Some corpses may give you intrinsic abilities when eaten, or increase your ability scores.
5. You should eat corpses that are safe to eat and which give good abilities. Do not eat dangerous corpses.
6. Do not eat corpses left by undead creatures, except wraith corpses.
7. Undead corpses are rotten, except for wraith corpses, which will give you an experience level.
8. Some fruits give you ability score bonuses, when eaten.
9. You can probe monsters and corpses with a wand of probing to determine what abilities they have and if the corpses are safe to eat.
10. Rings and amulets consume nutrition periodically when worn. If you are low on food, it may be wise not to wear them.
