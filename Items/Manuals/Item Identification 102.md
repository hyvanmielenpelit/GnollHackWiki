## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You can use a touchstone to identify gems.
2. You can use a wand of identify to identify items, 1 per charge.
3. You can cast the Identify spell to identify items.
4. You can cast the Detect Blessedness spell to determine the blessed/uncursed/cursed status of an item.
5. You can try to kick a gray stone, to see if it moves. If not, it is a cursed loadstone.
6. You can engrave with a wand a single letter on the ground, to see what it does.
7. You can see what the price of an item is in a shop and then try to figure it out, what the item is.
8. You can equip blessed and uncursed items to see what they do. Sometimes it is not obvious.
9. You can zap a wand of probing at corpses to see what they do when eaten.
