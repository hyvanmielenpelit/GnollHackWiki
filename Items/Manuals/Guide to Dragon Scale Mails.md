## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. If you polymorph while wearing a dragon scale mail, you will turn into a dragon and your dragon scale mail will revert to mere scales again.
2. You can turn dragon scales into a dragon scale mail by enchanting them or by asking a smith to forge them into a mail.
3. An orange dragon scale mail gives you sleep resistance and paralysis resistance.
4. A green dragon scale mail gives you poison resistance and sickness resistance.
5. A yellow dragon scale mail gives you acid resistance and petrification resistance.
6. A gray dragon scale mail gives you magic resistance.
7. A silver dragon scale mail gives you reflection.
8. A red dragon scale mail gives you full fire resistance, protection from green slime, and regeneration.
9. A white dragon scale mail gives you full cold resistance, protection from bisection, and energy regeneration.
10. A blue dragon scale mail gives you full shock resistance and very fast speed.
11. A black dragon scale mail gives you disintegration, death, and drain resistances.
