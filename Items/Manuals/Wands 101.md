## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You can zap wands at monsters and items.
2. You can zap wands even at yourself, as well as upwards and downwards.
3. You can also apply wands on an item. It applies the wand's effect on to the item.
4. For example, you can use a wand of cancellation to remove a curse from an item.
5. If you put a wand of cancellation or a Rod of Disjunction in a bag of holding, it will explode.
6. It is advisable to put wands of cancellation into ordinary bags to avoid putting them accidently into a magic bag.
7. You can zap a wand of digging downwards to create a hole to flee from a dangerous situation.
8. If you are engulfed by a monster, you can zap a wand of digging at it. It will spit you out and it is reduced to 1 HP.
9. A wand of create monster is useful for generating monsters for sacrificing at an altar.
10. Wands make a good offensive ranged weapon choice for classes that can use them well.
