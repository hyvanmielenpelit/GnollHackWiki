## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. You can dip potions twice into a fountain, moat, or a sink to dilute them into potions of water.
2. You can write scrolls and spellbooks with a magic marker.
3. You can wash scrolls and spellbooks clean by dipping them into a fountain, moat, or a sink.
4. You can use a pick-axe or a similar tool to dig through walls. You can also dig through the floor.
5. Disarming traps may generate useful items, such as darts or wands.
6. Searching can reveal secret doors. But it does not succeed always, so you should do it several times.
7. You can search chests for traps.
8. Engraving with a dagger or another weapon will make it dull, that is, reduce its enchantment.
9. Engraving a text with a wand may produce effects on bugs on the ground, which may help you identify the wand.
10. Engraving text with fingers is not permanent. The text will disappear soon.
11. Engraving with a dagger is a good way to make a permanent engraving. Just dispose the dagger afterwards.
