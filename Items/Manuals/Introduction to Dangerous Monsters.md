## Manual

- **Weight:** 2.0 lbs
- **Base value:** 10 gold
- **Base material:** Paper

## Description

This is a readable manual that provides useful information about various aspects of GnollHack.

## 📖 Contents

1. Hitting a floating eye in melee will paralyse you. Do not do it.
2. Watch out for nymphs and harpies. They can steal your items.
3. Leprechauns will steal your money that is in open inventory.
4. There are many dangerous monsters that should be killed from far away.
5. For example, werecreatures may bite you and cause you to contract lycanthropy.
6. Mummies cause mummy rot on touch. It prevents you from healing over time and reduces your constitution and charisma slowly.
7. Some snakes can constrict you. It is often a good idea to kill them from a distance.
8. Some monsters, such as owlbears, can grab you. You need to kill the grabbed monster first before doing anything else.
