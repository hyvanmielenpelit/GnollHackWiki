## Melee Weapon: Long Sword

- **Weight:** 3.8 lbs
- **Base value:** 1000 gold
- **Base material:** Silver
- **Skill:** Sword
- **Hands to use:** Single-handed
- **Base damage - Small:** 1d8 (avg 4.5, x2)
- **Base damage - Large:** 1d12 (avg 6.5, x2)
- **Silver bonus damage:** 1d20
- **Enchantable:** Yes
