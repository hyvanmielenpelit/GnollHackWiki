## Tool: Horn

- **Weight:** 1.1 lbs
- **Base value:** 50 gold
- **Base material:** Bone
- **Skill:** Wand
- **Item effect duration:** 1d10+70 rounds
- **Saving throw:** Against wisdom
- **Charges:** Yes
- **Maximum charges:** 8
- **Recharging type:** Rechargeable as musical instrument

## Description

When improvised, playing causes nearby monsters to become crazed, attacking each other.
