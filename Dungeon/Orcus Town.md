> 👉 **[[/Monsters/Orcus]] Town is a ruined, demonic reflection of a settlement, ruled by the demon lord [[/Monsters/Orcus]].**

**Location:** Located between levels 5 and 16 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]].

Orcus Town is the only recognizable "town" in the underworld.

## Characteristics

- **Layout:** The level features ruined buildings and abandoned shops, similar to [[/Dungeon/Mine Town]], but twisted and corrupted.
- **Inhabitants:** Instead of peaceful watchmen and [[shopkeepers|/Monsters/Shopkeeper]], the town is filled with undead, demons, and the powerful demon lord [[/Monsters/Orcus]].
- **Shops:** The abandoned shops often contain piles of uncursed (but abandoned) loot.

## [[/Monsters/Orcus]]

- He spawns with the artifact **[[/Artifacts/Wand of Orcus]]**. 
- He can also summon throngs of undead and cast powerful spells.
