> 👉 **[[/Monsters/Medusa]]'s Island is a dangerous, water-logged cavern housing the deadly gorgon, [[/Monsters/Medusa]].**

**Location:** Located 3 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]] (typically levels 21 to 23).

[[/Monsters/Medusa]]'s Island is a guaranteed encounter. 
*Message upon entering the level: "You hear the splashing of a naiad."*

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** The level consists of a small starting island and a larger main island across a body of water. 
- **Inhabitants:** The water is infested with [[giant eels|/Monsters/Giant eel]], [[/Monsters/Kraken]], and water [[trolls|/Monsters/Troll]]. On the main island resides [[/Monsters/Medusa]], accompanied by various snakes and amphibious monsters.
- **Statues:** The level is littered with statues of previous adventurers who looked at [[/Monsters/Medusa]] and were turned to stone.

## [[/Monsters/Medusa]]
[[/Monsters/Medusa]] possesses a passive gaze attack that will instantly turn you to stone if you look at her without reflection or a [[/Items/Blindfold]].
- **Defeating Her:** The easiest way to kill [[/Monsters/Medusa]] is to wear a source of reflection (like a [[/Items/Shield of reflection]] or an [[/Items/Amulet of reflection]]). When she looks at you, her gaze will reflect back, instantly petrifying her.
- **Alternative Methods:** You can fight her while blindfolded using telepathy, or polymorph into a monster with stoning resistance.
