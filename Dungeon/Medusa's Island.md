> 👉 **[[/Monsters/Medusa]]'s Island is a dangerous, water-logged cavern housing the deadly gorgon, [[/Monsters/Medusa]].**

**Location:** Located 3 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/Dungeons of Doom]] (typically levels 22–23).

[[/Monsters/Medusa]]'s Island is a guaranteed encounter. 
*Message upon entering the level: "You hear the splashing of a naiad."*

## ℹ️ Characteristics
- **Layout:** The level has four distinct map variants:
  - **Variant 1 & 2:** A small starting island and a larger main island across a body of water.
  - **Variant 3:** A sprawling archipelago of tiny islands covered in trees and swarming with ravens.
  - **Variant 4:** Medusa's palace, which includes a nesting [[yellow dragon|/Monsters/Yellow dragon]] in the backyard.
- **Inhabitants:** The water is infested with [[giant eels|/Monsters/Giant eel]], [[electric eels|/Monsters/Electric eel]], [[jellyfish|/Monsters/Jellyfish]], [[/Monsters/Kraken]], water [[trolls|/Monsters/Troll]], and [[water nymphs|/Monsters/Water nymph]]. On the islands reside [[/Monsters/Medusa]], accompanied by snakes, [[black nagas|/Monsters/Black naga]], [[stone golems|/Monsters/Stone golem]], [[ropers|/Monsters/Roper]], and various amphibians.
- **Flora:** The islands are dotted with various types of mushrooms and berries.
- **Statues:** The level is littered with statues of previous adventurers who looked at [[/Monsters/Medusa]] and were turned to stone. Notably, one of these statues is always **Perseus**, which has a high chance to contain a [[shield of reflection|/Items/Shield of reflection]] or [[levitation boots|/Items/Levitation boots]], along with a [[scimitar|/Items/Scimitar]] and a [[sack|/Items/Sack]].

## 🐍 [[/Monsters/Medusa]]
[[/Monsters/Medusa]] possesses a passive gaze attack that will instantly turn you to stone if you look at her without reflection or a [[/Items/Blindfold]].
- **Defeating Her:** The easiest way to kill [[/Monsters/Medusa]] is to wear a source of reflection (like a [[/Items/Shield of reflection]] or an [[/Items/Amulet of reflection]]). When she looks at you, her gaze will reflect back, instantly petrifying her.
- **Alternative Methods:** You can fight her while blindfolded using telepathy, or polymorph into a monster with stoning resistance.
