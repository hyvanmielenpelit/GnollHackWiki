> 👉 **The Plane of the Modron is a strange, geometric realm governed by absolute order, unique to GnollHack.**

**Location:** Accessible via a magic portal located between levels 3 and 5 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]]. The branch consists of 5 levels.

The Plane of the Modron introduces a highly structured, mechanical environment.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** The plane consists of 5 levels (Pemptonus, Tetartonus, Tritonus, Deuteronus, and Protonus). The architecture is rigidly geometric, reflecting the lawful and precise nature of its inhabitants.
- **Inhabitants:** Populated by [[Modrons|/Monsters/Modron]]—mechanical, geometric beings of pure law and order. 
- **Environment:** The rigid structure of the plane can make navigation predictable but heavily guarded.

## Special Qualities
- [[Modrons|/Monsters/Modron]] are relentless and organized. They attack in coordinated groups and utilize advanced tactics.
- The deepest level, Protonus, houses the highest-ranking [[Modrons|/Monsters/Modron]] and significant technological or magical loot.
