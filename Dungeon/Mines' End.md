> 👉 **Mines' End is the final, bottom-most level of the Gnomish Mines, housing significant treasure and the [[/Artifacts/Gladstone]].**

**Location:** Located at the very bottom of [[/Dungeon/The Gnomish Mines]] (level 8 to 10 of the branch, absolute depth 10 to 15).

Once you reach it, there are no stairs leading further down.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** Mines' End comes in several different variants (e.g., the Wine Cellar, the Mimic level, the Vampire level, or the Catacombs). 
- **Inhabitants:** The level is heavily guarded by powerful monsters relative to the early game, including [[mind flayers|/Monsters/Mind flayer]], [[vampires|/Monsters/Vampire]], or large numbers of [[mimics|/Monsters/Mimic]] depending on the variant.
- **Traps:** Often heavily trapped, particularly with polymorph traps.

## The Reward
The primary reason to venture to the bottom of the mines is the **[[/Artifacts/Gladstone]]**, a unique artifact luckstone. Having a non-cursed [[/Items/Luckstone]] in your inventory is crucial because it prevents your luck from timing out (returning to 0) over time, and grants a flat bonus to your luck score. High luck improves combat rolls, saving throws, and item generation.

Depending on generation, Mines' End may also contain a guaranteed alignment-specific artifact.
