# Ranger Quest

> 👉 **The Ranger Quest is a branch of [[/Dungeon/The Quest]] specifically designed for the Ranger role.**

## 🌍 Overview

The Quest branch for the Ranger consists of 5 or 6 levels. You must navigate through these levels to defeat your Nemesis and recover your role's quest artifact.

- **Leader:** [[/Monsters/Orion]] (found on the Home level)
- **Nemesis:** [[/Monsters/Scorpius]] (found on the Goal level)
- **Artifact:** [[/Artifacts/The Longbow of Diana]]
- **Common Enemies:** [[forest centaurs|/Monsters/Forest centaur]] and [[scorpions|/Monsters/Scorpion]]

## 🗺️ Level Structure

The Quest is divided into several specific levels:

### 1. Home Level
The starting point of the Quest. Here you will find [[/Monsters/Orion]] and friendly Quest Guardians. You must receive permission from [[/Monsters/Orion]] before proceeding deeper. The walls and floors are undiggable, and teleportation is restricted.

### 2. Upper Filler Level
An intermediate level filled with [[forest centaurs|/Monsters/Forest centaur]] and [[scorpions|/Monsters/Scorpion]] and other monsters.

### 3. Locate Level
A specific midpoint level. Upon entering, you will receive messages indicating that you are getting closer to [[/Monsters/Scorpius]].

### 4. Lower Filler Level(s)
One or two additional randomly generated levels before the final confrontation. (There is one lower filler level if the Quest is 5 levels deep, and two if it is 6 levels deep.)

### 5. Goal Level (Level 5 or 6)
The bottom-most level of the branch. Here you will face [[/Monsters/Scorpius]]. Upon defeating him, you can claim [[/Artifacts/The Longbow of Diana]] and the [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]].
