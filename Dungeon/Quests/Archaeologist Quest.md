# Archaeologist Quest

> 👉 **The Archaeologist Quest is a branch of the [[/Dungeon/Quest]] specifically designed for the Archaeologist role.**

## 🌍 Overview

The Quest branch for the Archaeologist consists of 5 or 6 levels. You must navigate through these levels to defeat your Nemesis and recover your role's quest artifact.

- **Leader:** [[/Monsters/Lord Carnarvon]] (found on the Home level)
- **Nemesis:** The [[Minion of Huhetotl|/Monsters/Minion of Huhetotl]] (found on the Goal level)
- **Artifact:** [[/Artifacts/The Orb of Detection]]
- **Common Enemies:** [[human mummies|/Monsters/Human mummy]]

## 🗺️ Level Structure

The Quest is divided into several specific levels:

### 1. Home Level

The starting point of the Quest. Here you will find [[/Monsters/Lord Carnarvon]] and friendly Quest Guardians. You must receive permission from [[/Monsters/Lord Carnarvon]] before proceeding deeper.

### 2. Upper Filler Level

An intermediate level filled with [[human mummies|/Monsters/Human mummy]] and other monsters.

### 3. Locate Level

A specific midpoint level. Upon entering, you will receive messages indicating that you are getting closer to The [[Minion of Huhetotl|/Monsters/Minion of Huhetotl]].

### 4. Lower Filler Level(s)

One or two additional randomly generated levels before the final confrontation. (There is one lower filler level if the Quest is 5 levels deep, and two if it is 6 levels deep.)

### 5. Goal Level (Level 5 or 6)

The bottom-most level of the branch. Here you will face The [[Minion of Huhetotl|/Monsters/Minion of Huhetotl]]. Upon defeating it, you can claim [[/Artifacts/The Orb of Detection]] and the [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]].
