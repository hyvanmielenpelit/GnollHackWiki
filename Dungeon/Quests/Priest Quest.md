# Priest Quest

> 👉 **The Priest Quest is a branch of [[/Dungeon/The Quest]] specifically designed for the Priest role.**

## Overview 🌍

The Quest branch for the Priest consists of 5 levels. You must navigate through these levels to defeat your Nemesis and recover your role's quest artifact.

- **Leader:** The [[Arch Priest|/Monsters/Arch Priest]] (found on the Home level)
- **Nemesis:** [[/Monsters/Nalzok]] (found on the Goal level)
- **Artifact:** [[/Artifacts/The Mitre of Holiness]]
- **Common Enemies:** [[human zombies|/Monsters/Human zombie]] and [[wraiths|/Monsters/Wraith]]

## Level Structure 🗺️

The Quest is divided into several specific levels:

### 1. Home Level
The starting point of the Quest. Here you will find The [[Arch Priest|/Monsters/Arch Priest]] and friendly Quest Guardians. You must receive permission from The [[Arch Priest|/Monsters/Arch Priest]] before proceeding deeper. The walls and floors are undiggable, and teleportation is restricted.

### 2. Upper Filler Level
An intermediate level filled with [[human zombies|/Monsters/Human zombie]] and [[wraiths|/Monsters/Wraith]] and other monsters.

### 3. Locate Level
A specific midpoint level. Upon entering, you will receive messages indicating that you are getting closer to [[/Monsters/Nalzok]].

### 4. Lower Filler Level
An additional randomly generated level before the final confrontation.

### 5. Goal Level
The bottom-most level of the branch. Here you will face [[/Monsters/Nalzok]]. Upon defeating it, you can claim [[/Artifacts/The Mitre of Holiness]] and the [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]].
