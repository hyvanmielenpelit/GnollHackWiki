# Wizard Quest

> 👉 **The Wizard Quest is a branch of [[/Dungeon/The Quest]] specifically designed for the Wizard role.**

## Overview 🌍

The Quest branch for the Wizard consists of 5 levels. You must navigate through these levels to defeat your Nemesis and recover your role's quest artifact.

- **Leader:** [[/Monsters/Neferet the Green]] (found on the Home level)
- **Nemesis:** The [[Dark One|/Monsters/Dark One]] (found on the Goal level)
- **Artifact:** [[/Artifacts/The Eye of the Aethiopica]]
- **Common Enemies:** [[vampire bats|/Monsters/Vampire bat]] and [[xorns|/Monsters/Xorn]]

## Level Structure 🗺️

The Quest is divided into several specific levels:

### 1. Home Level
The starting point of the Quest. Here you will find [[/Monsters/Neferet the Green]] and friendly Quest Guardians. You must receive permission from [[/Monsters/Neferet the Green]] before proceeding deeper. The walls and floors are undiggable, and teleportation is restricted.

### 2. Upper Filler Level
An intermediate level filled with [[vampire bats|/Monsters/Vampire bat]] and [[xorns|/Monsters/Xorn]] and other monsters.

### 3. Locate Level
A specific midpoint level. Upon entering, you will receive messages indicating that you are getting closer to The [[Dark One|/Monsters/Dark One]].

### 4. Lower Filler Level
An additional randomly generated level before the final confrontation.

### 5. Goal Level
The bottom-most level of the branch. Here you will face The [[Dark One|/Monsters/Dark One]]. Upon defeating him, you can claim [[/Artifacts/The Eye of the Aethiopica]] and the [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]].
