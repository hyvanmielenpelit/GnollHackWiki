> 👉 **Mine Town is a bustling underground settlement in the Gnomish Mines, offering shops and a temple to early-game characters.**

**Location:** Located between levels 3 and 4 of [[/Dungeon/The Gnomish Mines]] (absolute depth 4–7).

Mine Town is a guaranteed special level. 
*Message: You know you have entered Mine Town if you hear the sound of a cash register, or the presence of [[town watchmen|/Monsters/Watchman]].*

## 🏘️ Characteristics
- **Layout:** A large open cavern containing several enclosed buildings with wooden doors. The town is fully lit, and there are 7 different map variants (including "Frontier Town", "Bazaar", and others).
- **Inhabitants:** Usually heavily patrolled by peaceful [[town watchmen|/Monsters/Watchman]] and a [[/Monsters/Watch captain]]. The cavern surrounding the town is filled with standard Mines enemies ([[gnomes|/Monsters/Gnome]] and [[dwarves|/Monsters/Dwarf]]).
- **Amenities:** 
  - A guaranteed Temple with an [[aligned priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]] (useful for buying protection and identifying BUC status of items).
  - A lighting store (sells [[lamps|/Items/Oil lamp]] and candles).
  - A general store, and typically one or more specialized stores (like a bookstore or delicatessen).

## 👹 Orcish Town

One of the 7 Mine Town variants is the much-dreaded **Orcish Town**. If generated:
- The town has been overrun by an orcish army.
- The watchmen and shopkeepers are dead.
- The temple is converted to an unaligned altar with an [[orc shaman|/Monsters/Orc shaman]].
- The shops are ruined, their doors are gone, and only scattered loot remains.

### 🆕 GnollHack Additions

- A peaceful orc hermit can be found living in one of the rooms.
- A special buried, trapped chest can be found containing a [[shirt of sound mindedness|/Items/Shirt of sound mindedness]], [[sunglasses|/Items/Sunglasses]], and a ring.
- In the temple, a heavy, locked chest requiring a bronze key holds a guaranteed orc artifact, along with gold, gems, and weapons.
