> 👉 **The Valley of the Dead is a sprawling, undead-filled graveyard that marks the transition into Gehennom.**

**Location:** The top-most level (level 1) of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]].

Located immediately below the [[/Dungeon/Underground Castle]], you cannot enter Gehennom without passing through it.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** A massive, open valley filled with dozens of graves and three distinct temples.
- **Inhabitants:** The valley is swarming with undead: [[liches|/Monsters/Lich]], [[vampires|/Monsters/Vampire]], [[ghosts|/Monsters/Ghost]], wrights, and [[zombies|/Monsters/Zombie]]. 
- **The Temples:** There are three temples on the level, one for each alignment (Lawful, Neutral, Chaotic). Each is attended by an [[aligned priest|/Monsters/Aligned priest]] of Moloch.
