> 👉 **The Elemental Planes form the perilous endgame sequence of GnollHack, a final gauntlet leading to ascension.**

**Location:** Accessed via the staircase up on dungeon level 1 after obtaining the Amulet of Yendor. Consists of 5 levels.

After securing the Amulet of Yendor from [[/Dungeon/Moloch's Sanctum]] and performing the invocation, players must ascend back up through the dungeon to level 1 and take the staircase up.

## 🪜 The Sequence

The player must traverse five distinct planes in strict order:

1. [Plane of Earth](#plane-of-earth)
2. [Plane of Air](#plane-of-air)
3. [Plane of Fire](#plane-of-fire)
4. [Plane of Water](#plane-of-water)
5. [The Astral Plane](#the-astral-plane)

## 🪨 Plane of Earth

> 👉 **A claustrophobic realm of solid rock where players must dig their way forward.**

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Teleportation is disabled, visibility is reduced, and digging down is impossible. |
| **Layout** | Several cavernous pockets separated by solid, diggable rock. Uses the Gnomish Mines tileset. |
| **Exit** | A magic portal randomly hidden within the solid rock, leading to the [Plane of Air](#plane-of-air). |

### Inhabitants and Items

- **Notable Encounters:**
  - An [[/Monsters/Elvenking]] and a [[/Monsters/Minotaur]].
  - A [[/Monsters/Dwarf king]] equipped with a [[/Items/Dwarvish axe]], [[/Items/Dwarvish helm]], [[/Items/Dwarvish roundshield]], [[/Items/Dwarvish mithril-coat]], a [[/Items/Pick-axe]], and a [[/Items/Scroll of magic mapping]].
  - Two [[/Monsters/Barbed devil]]s, one carrying a [[/Items/Wand of digging]] and the other a [[/Items/Scroll of magic mapping]].
- **Common Monsters:**
  - Dominated by [[/Monsters/Earth elemental]] and [[/Monsters/Elder earth elemental]].
  - Also features [[/Monsters/Rock troll]], [[/Monsters/Stone giant]], [[/Monsters/Stone golem]], [[/Monsters/Umbral hulk]], [[/Monsters/Dust vortex]], [[/Monsters/Pit viper]], and scorpions.

## 💨 Plane of Air

> 👉 **A vast, gravity-free expanse of clouds filled with fast-moving flying entities.**

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Teleportation is disabled, visibility is reduced, and digging down is impossible. There is no gravity, making levitation or flying mandatory. |
| **Layout** | An open expanse consisting entirely of clouds. The player arrives on the left side of the map. |
| **Exit** | A magic portal randomly placed on the right side of the map, leading to the [Plane of Fire](#plane-of-fire). |

### Inhabitants

- **Monsters:**
  - Dominated by [[/Monsters/Air elemental]] and [[/Monsters/Elder air elemental]].
  - Also features [[/Monsters/Floating eye]], [[/Monsters/Yellow light]], [[/Monsters/Couatl]], dragons, [[/Monsters/Jabberwock]], [[/Monsters/Djinni]], [[/Monsters/Fog cloud]], [[/Monsters/Energy vortex]], and [[/Monsters/Steam vortex]].

## 🔥 Plane of Fire

> 👉 **A searing, volcanic landscape with flowing lava lakes and hidden traps.**

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Teleportation is disabled, visibility is reduced, and digging down is impossible. |
| **Layout** | A wide-open space with numerous lava lakes and exactly 40 randomly placed fire traps. |
| **Exit** | A magic portal randomly placed anywhere on the map, leading to the [Plane of Water](#plane-of-water). |

### Inhabitants

- **Monsters:**
  - Dominated by [[/Monsters/Fire elemental]] and [[/Monsters/Elder fire elemental]].
  - Also features [[/Monsters/Red dragon]], [[/Monsters/Balor]], [[/Monsters/Fire giant]], [[/Monsters/Barbed devil]], [[/Monsters/Hell hound]], [[/Monsters/Stone golem]], [[/Monsters/Pit fiend]], [[/Monsters/Pit viper]], [[/Monsters/Salamander]], [[/Monsters/Dust vortex]], [[/Monsters/Fire vortex]], and [[/Monsters/Steam vortex]].

## 💧 Plane of Water

> 👉 **A submerged ocean realm where players and sea creatures reside inside shifting air bubbles.**

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Teleportation is disabled, visibility is reduced, and digging down is impossible. |
| **Layout** | A fully submerged level filled with water. The player and monsters reside inside shifting air bubbles. Movement outside bubbles causes drowning unless the player has magical breathing. |
| **Exit** | A magic portal located in an air bubble on the right side of the level, leading to [The Astral Plane](#the-astral-plane). |

### Inhabitants

- **Monsters:**
  - Dominated by [[/Monsters/Water elemental]] and [[/Monsters/Elder water elemental]].
  - Also features [[/Monsters/Giant eel]], [[/Monsters/Electric eel]], [[/Monsters/Kraken]], [[/Monsters/Shark]], [[/Monsters/Piranha]], and [[/Monsters/Jellyfish]].

## 🌌 The Astral Plane

To win the game, you must find the high altar belonging to your god and offer the [[/Items/Amulet of Yendor]].

| Feature | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Environment** | Teleportation, magic mapping, digging, and passing through walls are completely disabled. Visibility is reduced. |
| **Layout** | Three lit courts containing High Altars (one Lawful, one Neutral, and one Chaotic). The alignments of the shrines are randomly shuffled every game. |
| **Special Generation** | There is a high chance for the bottom-center area to generate additional rooms containing extra angels and monsters. |

### Inhabitants and Items

- **Notable Encounters:**
  - The three Riders guard the shrine entrances (their locations are randomly shuffled): [[/Monsters/Death]], [[/Monsters/Famine]], and [[/Monsters/Pestilence]].
  - A guaranteed [[/Items/Magic chest]] spawns in the center area.
  - Aligned priests ([[/Monsters/Aligned priest]]) and angels ([[/Monsters/Angel]]) guard the courts and shrines.
- **Common Monsters:**
  - Angels ([[/Monsters/Angel]]), liches, vampires, and dragons.
