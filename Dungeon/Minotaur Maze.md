> 👉 **The Minotaur Maze is a confusing, highly lethal labyrinth located near the bottom of the Dungeons of Doom, New in GnollHack.**

**Location:** Located 2 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]] (typically levels 22 to 24).

Situated just above the [[/Dungeon/Underground Castle]], the Minotaur Maze is designed to drain the player's resources before the mid-game finale.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** A dense, sprawling maze with no standard rooms. The walls are solid rock.
- **Inhabitants:** As the name suggests, the level is heavily populated by [[minotaurs|/Monsters/Minotaur]]. 
- **Traps:** Riddled with hidden traps, particularly teleport traps that disorient the player and drop them into groups of enemies.

## The Threat of [[Minotaurs|/Monsters/Minotaur]]
[[Minotaurs|/Monsters/Minotaur]] are some of the most dangerous melee enemies in the game. They dual-wield weapons, move incredibly fast, and hit hard enough to kill unprepared characters in a single turn.
