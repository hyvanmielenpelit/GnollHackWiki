> 👉 **The Minotaur Maze is a confusing, highly lethal labyrinth located near the bottom of the Dungeons of Doom, New in GnollHack.**

**Location:** Located 2 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]] (typically levels 23–24).

Situated just above the [[/Dungeon/Underground Castle]], the Minotaur Maze is designed to drain the player's resources before the mid-game finale.

## Characteristics 🐂
- **Layout:** A dense, sprawling maze with no standard rooms. The walls are solid rock. Hidden within the maze are two specific structures: the Minotaur's Lair and a locked NPC room.
- **Inhabitants:** As the name suggests, the level contains a [[/Monsters/minotaur]].

## The Minotaur's Lair 🗝️

On the left side of the maze is the Minotaur's Lair, housing a trapped and locked chest, whic contains a large amount of gold, a chance for an artifact, and a crucial [[magic key|/Items/Magic key]].

## The Geologist ⛏️

On the right side of the maze is a small room with parquet flooring, secured by a locked, windowed magic door. This door can be unlocked using the magic key found in the Minotaur's Lair. Inside resides a [[/Monsters/dwarven geologist]] NPC who can provide useful services.

## The Threat ⚠️

[[/Monsters/Minotaur]] is a dangerous melee enemy in the game. It moves fast and hits hard and can kill an unprepared adventurer in a few turns.
