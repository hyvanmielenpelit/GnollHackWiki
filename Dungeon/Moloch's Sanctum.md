> 👉 **Moloch's Sanctum is the deepest, most dangerous level of Gehennom, housing the [[/Artifacts/Amulet of Yendor]].**

**Location:** The bottom-most level of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]] (typically Gehennom level 18 to 21).

It cannot be accessed by normal stairs; the only way to enter is by performing the Invocation ritual on the vibrating square.

## Entry Requirements
To access the Sanctum, you must possess three unique items:
1. The **[[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]]** (from [[/Dungeon/The Quest]])
2. The **[[/Artifacts/Candelabrum of Invocation]]** (from [[/Dungeon/Vlad's Tower]])
3. The **[[/Artifacts/Book of the Dead]]** (from the [[/Dungeon/Wizard's Tower]])

You must locate the vibrating square on the lowest regular level of Gehennom, ring the Bell, light the Candelabrum, and read the [[/Artifacts/Book of the Dead]] to reveal the staircase down.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** A cavernous area surrounded by fire and lava, with a massive unpassable wall enclosing the Sanctum itself.
- **Inhabitants:** The entire level is swarming with high-level [[demons|/Monsters/Demon]], elemental enemies, and the [[/Monsters/High Priest of Moloch]].
- **The Sanctum:** The inner sanctum is protected by a secret door. Inside sits the [[/Monsters/High Priest of Moloch]], who holds the [[/Artifacts/Amulet of Yendor]].
