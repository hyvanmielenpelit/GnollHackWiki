> 👉 **Dis, Maladomini, and [[/Monsters/Jubilex]]'s Realm are the domains of powerful demon lords in Gehennom.**

**Location:** Dis is located exactly on level 2 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]].

As you navigate the endless mazes of Gehennom, you will inevitably stumble upon the lairs of specific named demon lords.

## [[/Monsters/Dispater]] in Dis
- **Layout:** Dis is the iron city. It often features iron bars and specialized structures.
- **[[/Monsters/Dispater]]:** [[/Monsters/Dispater]] is the ruler of Dis. He is a powerful arch-fiend capable of casting spells and summoning other [[demons|/Monsters/Demon]]. He often wields a unique wand.

## [[/Monsters/Baalzebub]] in Maladomini
- **Layout:** Maladomini is a dark, corrupted realm.
- **[[/Monsters/Baalzebub]]:** The lord of flies. He is highly dangerous and can quickly overwhelm the player with summoned swarms and demonic allies.

## [[/Monsters/Jubilex]]'s Realm
- **Layout:** A swampy, disgusting lair filled with pools of water and acidic sludge.
- **[[/Monsters/Jubilex]]:** The faceless lord of [[oozes|/Monsters/Ooze]]. [[/Monsters/Jubilex]] does not summon [[demons|/Monsters/Demon]] but can engulf the player, causing massive damage and illness. He is incredibly slow but extremely deadly in melee combat.
