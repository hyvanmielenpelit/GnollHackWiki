> 👉 **Dis is a hellish fortress located in the upper levels of Gehennom, ruled by the arch-devil [[/Monsters/Dispater]].**

**Location:** Dis is located exactly on level 2 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]].

## 🏰 Characteristics

Dis is explicitly known as "A hellish castle". Unlike the standard mazes of Gehennom, Dis features a fixed layout representing a heavily fortified demonic stronghold.

- **The Grand Entrance:** The player arrives at the beginning of a long entrance corridor lined with a red carpet. The path is illuminated by braziers and flanked by statues of various devils (pit fiends, erinys, horned devils, ice devils, and barbed devils). A signpost at the entrance ominously warns: "Uninvited Visitors Not Welcome". The entrance culminates at a locked black gate made of obsidian.
- **The Fortress Interior:** Beyond the gate lies the main fortress, which is entirely non-diggable. It contains several specialized rooms:
  - **Dispater's Throne Room:** The central chamber where Dispater holds court, surrounded by statues of erinys.
  - **Library:** A room containing multiple bookshelves.
  - **Armories:** Rooms featuring weapon racks and locked chests filled with weapons and armor.
  - **Reagent Rooms:** Small and large storage rooms containing various gems and rocks.
- **Traps:** The fortress is heavily trapped, featuring spiked pits, fire traps, sleep gas, anti-magic traps, and magic traps.

## 👹 Inhabitants

The fortress is heavily guarded by the denizens of hell:
- Numerous devils patrol the level, including horned devils, pit fiends, and erinys.
- Liches are also known to spawn within the castle walls.

## 😈 [[/Monsters/Dispater]]

[[/Monsters/Dispater]], the Archduke of Gehennom, is the level boss. He is a powerful arch-devil capable of casting spells and summoning other devils.

- **Dispater's Treasure:** In his throne room lies a trapped and locked chest that can only be opened with the ornamental key. This chest contains a massive amount of gold, a guaranteed [[/Items/scroll of supreme diabolism]], and a high chance of containing an artifact, along with other valuable loot.
