> 👉 **The Elemental Planes form the perilous endgame sequence of GnollHack, a final gauntlet leading to ascension.**

**Location:** Accessed via a portal on dungeon level 1 after obtaining the [[/Artifacts/Amulet of Yendor]]. Consists of 6 levels.

After securing the [[/Artifacts/Amulet of Yendor]] from [[/Dungeon/Moloch's Sanctum]] and performing the invocation, players must ascend back up through the dungeon to level 1 to reach this portal.

## The Sequence
The player must traverse five distinct planes in strict order:

1. **Plane of Earth:** A maze of solid rock. You must dig your way out while fighting earth [[elementals|/Monsters/Elemental]], [[minotaurs|/Monsters/Minotaur]], and the [[/Monsters/Wizard of Yendor]] (who will constantly harass you).
2. **Plane of Air:** An open expanse of clouds with no solid ground. You must levitate or fly to survive while battling air [[elementals|/Monsters/Elemental]] and dangerous flying monsters.
3. **Plane of Fire:** A landscape dominated by lava. Fire resistance and levitation are mandatory. Fire [[elementals|/Monsters/Elemental]] and powerful [[demons|/Monsters/Demon]] swarm you.
4. **Plane of Water:** You are submerged in an endless ocean. Magical breathing or water-walking is required to survive against water [[elementals|/Monsters/Elemental]] and [[/Monsters/Kraken]].
5. **The Astral Plane:** The final challenge. A vast heavenly battleground featuring three high altars and hordes of powerful angels. 

## The Astral Plane
To win the game, you must find the high altar belonging to your god and offer the [[/Artifacts/Amulet of Yendor]]. 
- There are three altars (Lawful, Neutral, Chaotic), but only one belongs to your god.
- You must identify your altar while fighting through the angelic hosts of the Riders (Death, Famine, and Pestilence).
