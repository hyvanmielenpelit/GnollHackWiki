> 👉 **The Elemental Planes form the perilous endgame sequence of GnollHack, a final gauntlet leading to ascension.**

**Location:** Accessed via the staircase up on dungeon level 1 after obtaining the Amulet of Yendor. Consists of 5 levels.

After securing the Amulet of Yendor from [[/Dungeon/Moloch's Sanctum]] and performing the invocation, players must ascend back up through the dungeon to level 1 and take the staircase up.

## The Sequence 🪜
The player must traverse five distinct planes in strict order:

1. **Plane of Earth:** Solid rock. You must dig your way out while fighting earth elementals.
2. **Plane of Air:** An open expanse of clouds with no solid ground. You must levitate or fly to survive while battling air elementals and dangerous flying monsters.
3. **Plane of Fire:** A landscape dominated by lava. Fire resistance and levitation are mandatory. Fire elementals and powerful demons swarm you.
4. **Plane of Water:** You are submerged in an ocean.
5. **The Astral Plane:** The final challenge.

## The Astral Plane 🌌

To win the game, you must find the high altar belonging to your god and offer the [[/Items/Amulet of Yendor]]. 
- There are three altars (Lawful, Neutral, Chaotic), but only one belongs to your god.
- You must identify your altar while fighting through the angelic hosts of the Riders ([[/Monsters/Death]], [[/Monsters/Famine]], and [[/Monsters/Pestilence]]).
