> 👉 **The Fake Wizard's Towers are two nearly identical decoy structures found deep in Gehennom. One holds the magic portal leading to the true [[/Dungeon/Wizard's Tower]], while the other holds a randomly generated amulet.**

**Location:** Both towers are generated between levels 13 and 18 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]] (specifically, between 2 and 5 levels above the bottom of Gehennom). 

They cannot be accessed by normal stairs from the outside.

## 🚪 Entry

Both fake towers appear from the outside as small structures surrounded by a moat. There is no way to tell which of the two towers is which from the outside. You must find a way past the moat and through the solid rock walls to discover what is inside. 

Once inside, one of the two towers contains a magic portal that transports you directly to the bottom level of the true [[/Dungeon/Wizard's Tower]]. The other tower contains a randomly generated amulet. 

## ℹ️ Characteristics

Both towers share the exact same physical characteristics and inhabitants:
- **Layout:** A very small 3x3 interior room surrounded by an impassable moat and non-diggable solid rock walls. 
- **Monsters:** Each tower is guaranteed to be guarded by a [[/Monsters/lich]], a [[/Monsters/vampire lord]], and a [[/Monsters/kraken]] lurking in the moat.
- **Hazards:** The small room contains multiple squeaky board traps. 
- **Decorations:** The north wall of the interior room always features a painting of either a blue or red wizard.
