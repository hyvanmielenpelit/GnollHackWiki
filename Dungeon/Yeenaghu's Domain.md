> 👉 **[[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]]'s Domain is the ruined, savage realm of the demon lord of [[gnolls|/Monsters/Gnoll]], exclusive to GnollHack.**

**Location:** Located between 2 and 9 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]] (typically Gehennom levels 9 to 19).

[[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]]'s Domain is a unique special level that serves as a thematic cornerstone of GnollHack.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** A savage, bone-littered wasteland reeking of death and decay.
- **Inhabitants:** The level is swarming with high-level [[gnolls|/Monsters/Gnoll]], demonic hyenas, and other savage beasts devoted to [[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]].
- **[[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]]:** The Demon Lord of Gnolls is a brutal melee combatant who attacks with relentless ferocity and wields a signature artifact flail.
