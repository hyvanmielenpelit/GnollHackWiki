> 👉 **[[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]]'s Domain is the ruined, savage realm of the demon lord of [[gnolls|/Monsters/Gnoll]], exclusive to GnollHack.**

**Location:** Located between 2 and 8 levels above the bottom of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]] (typically Gehennom levels 10–18).

Yeenaghu's Domain is a unique special level that serves as a thematic cornerstone of GnollHack.

## ℹ️ Characteristics
- **Layout:** The level features a hellish stronghold adorned with red carpets and banners. It contains barracks and a working smithy run by a [[/Monsters/flind lord]].
- **Inhabitants:** The domain is heavily guarded by Yeenaghu's most loyal followers, including [[shoosuvas|/Monsters/Shoosuva]], [[flinds|/Monsters/Flind]], [[flind lords|/Monsters/Flind lord]], and [[thouls|/Monsters/Thoul]].

## 🐺 [[/Monsters/Yeenaghu]]

The Demon Lord of Gnolls resides in the stronghold. He is a brutal melee combatant who attacks with relentless ferocity. Defeating him yields a special ornamental key.
