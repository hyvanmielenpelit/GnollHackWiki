> 👉 **The Large Circular Dungeon is a high-tech, quantum-themed branch accessible from the Gnomish Mines, exclusive to GnollHack.**

**Location:** Accessible via a magic portal located 1 to 2 levels below [[/Dungeon/Mine Town]] (between levels 4 and 6 of the Gnomish Mines, or absolute depth 5–9). The branch consists of 2 levels.

It features unique mechanics and dangerous futuristic enemies.

## Characteristics ⚛️

- **Levels:** Consists of two levels: the Quantum Tunnel (level 1) and the Quantum Core (level 2).
- **Layout:** The dungeon is highly structured and heavily utilizes circular motifs, contrasting sharply with the medieval fantasy setting of the rest of the game.
- **Inhabitants:** Populated by [[quantum mechanics|/Monsters/Quantum mechanic]] and [[elder quantum mechanics|/Monsters/Elder quantum mechanic]]. The Quantum Core can be exceedingly dangerous for low-level characters, because [[tentacled ones|/Monsters/Tentacled one]] and [[elder tentacled ones|/Monsters/Elder tentacled one]] can appear there.

## Special Qualities & Loot 📖

The Large Circular Dungeon contains significant amounts of incredibly rare, high-tier loot. If you can survive the Quantum Core, you may find powerful wands (such as teleportation, cancellation, and disintegration), a [[cubic gate|/Items/Cubic gate]], and legendary spellbooks — including the spell of [[/Spells/Sphere of annihilation]], [[/Spells/Black blade of disaster]], and even the fabled spellbook of [[/Spells/wish]].
