> 👉 **The Big Room is a notorious, massive, single-room level known for its high danger and lack of cover.**

**Location:** Located between levels 10 and 13 of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]].

The Big Room is exactly what it sounds like: a single, giant room that takes up almost the entire map.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** One gigantic room. There are no corridors, doors, or chokepoints.
- **Inhabitants:** Because of the open space, the level spawns a very high density of monsters. 
- **Line of Sight:** Every monster on the level can see you almost immediately upon entering, meaning spellcasters, breath weapons, and ranged attackers will all target you simultaneously.
