> 👉 **Maladomini is the dark, corrupted domain of [[/Monsters/Baalzebub]], the Lord of the Flies.**

**Location:** Located between levels 4 and 8 of [[/Dungeon/Gehennom]].

Found within the sprawling mazes of the underworld, Maladomini is a unique special level housing one of the major demon lords.

## ℹ️ Characteristics
- **Layout:** A grand but hellish mansion built into the rock.
- **The Court:** The mansion is guarded by a host of powerful demons including [[pit fiends|/Monsters/Pit fiend]], [[sandestins|/Monsters/Sandestin]], [[erinys|/Monsters/Erinys]], and [[lemures|/Monsters/Lemure]]. 
- **The Corrupted Kings:** A unique feature of this level is the presence of five corrupted kings representing the playable races: Orodhuil the Black-Hearted ([[elvenking|/Monsters/Elvenking]]), Burin the Wicked ([[dwarf king|/Monsters/Dwarf king]]), Hoggen II ([[gnoll king|/Monsters/Gnoll king]]), Varamir the Terrible ([[human king|/Monsters/Human king]]), and Obuld I Longfangs ([[orc king|/Monsters/Orc king]]). They are heavily armed with enchanted and legendary gear.

## 🪰 [[/Monsters/Baalzebub]]
[[/Monsters/Baalzebub]] is highly dangerous. He is fast, hits hard, and is capable of a stunning gaze and a poisonous bite. He can quickly overwhelm the player with summoned swarms of insects and demonic allies. He guards a special trapped chest containing a large amount of gold, a [[scroll of supreme diabolism|/Items/Scroll of supreme diabolism]], and other valuable loot.
