> 👉 **Delphi is a special level in the early Dungeons of Doom, serving as the home of the [[/Monsters/Oracle]].**

**Location:** Located between levels 5 and 8 of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]].

Delphi is a guaranteed, fixed-layout level.

## Characteristics
- **Layout:** The level is mostly solid rock, dominated by a large, central open area containing the [[/Monsters/Oracle]]'s temple. 
- **The [[/Monsters/Oracle]]:** In the very center sits the [[/Monsters/Oracle]], a peaceful NPC. She is surrounded by statues of centaurs (or living centaurs, depending on generation). 
- **Wooded Areas:** The temple is often surrounded by trees and pools of water, making it visually distinct.

## The [[/Monsters/Oracle]]'s Services
You can pay the [[/Monsters/Oracle]] for consultations, which come in two forms:
- **Minor Consultations:** Costs 50 gold. The [[/Monsters/Oracle]] provides a piece of standard, generalized advice (often cryptic rumors).
- **Major Consultations:** Costs 1,000 gold. The [[/Monsters/Oracle]] provides true, verified information about the game's mechanics or deep secrets.

Paying the [[/Monsters/Oracle]] is completely optional but can be useful for identifying items or learning obscure mechanics if you are playing without external guides.
