> 👉 **Delphi is a special level in the early Dungeons of Doom, serving as the home of the [[/Monsters/Oracle]].**

**Location:** Located between levels 5 and 8 of [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]].

Delphi is a guaranteed level with a partially fixed central room, but otherwise randomly generated rooms and corridors.

## Characteristics 🏛️

- **Layout:** The level consists of standard randomly generated rooms and corridors, but it always features a large, central open room containing the [[/Monsters/Oracle]]'s temple. 
- **The Temple:** In the very center sits the [[/Monsters/Oracle]], a peaceful NPC. She is surrounded by four distinct magical fountains (Healing, Magic, Water, and Mana). The temple room also contains statues of four centaurs and four elementals (Fire, Water, Earth, Air).

## The [[/Monsters/Oracle]]'s Services

You can pay the [[/Monsters/Oracle]] for consultations, which come in two forms:
- **Minor Consultations:** Costs 50 gold. The [[/Monsters/Oracle]] provides a piece of standard, generalized advice.
- **Major Consultations:** Costs 1,000 gold. The [[/Monsters/Oracle]] provides true, verified information about the game's mechanics or deep secrets.

Paying the [[/Monsters/Oracle]] is completely optional but can be useful for identifying items or learning obscure mechanics if you are playing without external guides.
