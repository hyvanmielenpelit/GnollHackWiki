> 👉 **The Quest is a role-specific branch where the player returns to their homeland to defeat their nemesis, retrieve their class artifact, and earn the [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]].**

**Location:** Branches from [[/Dungeon/The Dungeons of Doom]] via a magic portal between levels 11 and 16. The branch is 5 to 6 levels deep.

*Message upon entering the portal level: "You feel an almost forgotten tug..."*

## Entry Requirements 📜

To step through the portal and enter the Quest, you must meet three strict conditions:
1. **Experience Level 14:** You must be at least level 14. If you are lower, the Quest Leader will turn you away, and the portal will not activate.
2. **Alignment:** Your alignment record must be positive. You must behave in a manner consistent with your god's teachings.
3. **Approval:** You must visit your Quest Leader on the Home level and receive their blessing.

## Characteristics 🌍

The Quest consists of 5 or 6 levels, entirely tailored to your character's role (e.g., Valkyrie, Wizard, Tourist). 

- **Home Level:** The starting point, featuring your Quest Leader, friendly guards, and usually a temple.
- **Upper/Lower Filler Levels:** Intermediate levels filled with role-specific monsters.
- **Locate Level:** A specific midpoint level where the flavor text indicates you are getting closer to your Nemesis.
- **Goal Level:** The bottom-most level, housing your Nemesis.

## The Nemesis and The Reward 🏆

At the bottom of the branch, you must defeat your role's Nemesis (e.g., Lord Surtur for Valkyries, the Dark One for Wizards). The Nemesis is a powerful, unique monster. 

Upon defeating the Nemesis, you are rewarded with:
- **Your Role's Quest Artifact:** An incredibly powerful, unique item that defines your class's endgame capabilities (e.g., The Orb of Fate, The Eye of the Aethiopica).
- **The [[/Artifacts/Bell of Opening]]:** One of the three invocation items required to enter [[/Dungeon/Moloch's Sanctum]] and complete the game.
