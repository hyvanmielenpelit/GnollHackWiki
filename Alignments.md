GnollHack has the following alignments:
- [[/Lawful]] — Worships a good deity, who penalizes for both minor and major misdeeds
- [[/Neutral]] — Worships a neutral deity, who penalizes for major misdeeds
- [[/Chaotic]] — Worships an evil deity, who doesn't penalize for most misdeeds
