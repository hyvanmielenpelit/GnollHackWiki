GnollHack has the following alignments:
- [[/Lawful]] — The character worships a good deity, who penalizes for both minor and major misdeeds
- [[/Neutral]] — The character worships a neutral deity, who penalizes for major misdeeds
- [[/Chaotic]] — The character worships an evil deity, who doesn't penalize for most misdeeds
