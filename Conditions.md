# General Conditions
## Blind
### Symbol
![condition_blind](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/983e2ff0-713a-42fa-b26e-3f2dc219070e)
### Effect
The character cannot see, except via blind telepathy.


## Cancelled
### Symbol
![condition_cancl](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e6c7d92c-f70c-46cf-90ae-f930cfa23af7)
### Effect
The character cannot cast spells or use wands.


## Confused
### Symbol
![condition_conf](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/18ceadcd-ea1a-4960-be67-ca9144906a80)
### Effect
The character may move in a random direction. The character will also miscast spells and misread scrolls.


## Deaf
### Symbol
![condition_deaf](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a89f2fa-b6cb-4b39-b7dc-99d3ae1b5115)
### Effect
The character cannot hear. Sounds dependent on hearing will stop playing as long as the character is deaf.


## Fear
### Symbol
![condition_fear](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/77fa5eed-a2e2-495c-be43-1b3e38f25a46)
### Effect (Monsters Only)
The monster will flee.


## Flying
### Symbol
![condition_fly](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/330dabae-d94e-4534-9a28-86515f76fe9c)
### Effect
The character flies but can also access items on the ground, opposite to levitation.


## Food Poisoning
### Symbol
![condition_foodpois](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/365116ca-00f7-44c6-8a32-98aa0b89c507)
### Effect
The character will die in 10–19 turns.


## Grabbed
### Symbol
![condition_grab](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fe56180a-7dc6-4548-a905-870433430b99)
### Effect
The character cannot move away from the grabbing monster. An attempt to do so wastes a turn.


## Hallucinating
### Symbol
![condition_hallu](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d7322433-21a9-427d-a5f9-1e5023659bed)
### Effect
The character sees things as random monsters and items.


## Levitating
### Symbol
![condition_lev](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4b33a9cd-f8b7-4f9d-9ca1-dcfdc8c4929b)
### Effect
The character levitates in the air and cannot reach ground. Firing a missile weapon will make the character to move in the opposite direction.


## Lycanthropy
### Symbol
![condition_lyca](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0556c81e-b08e-4791-a1a7-846fa5f161f5)
### Effect
The character is infected with lycanthropy, making him or her into a were-animal, which turns into the animal form at random times. In the wereform, the character can summon animals of the same kind for help.


## Mummy Rot
### Symbol
![condition_rot](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8dee4b8e-691f-4a3a-8c36-76b916153aaf)
### Effect
The character is infected with mummy rot, which prevents the character from healing and reduces his or her constitution and charisma over time.


## Paralyzed
### Symbol
![condition_paral](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/20a20358-053b-41b7-80eb-1075f7f07bae)
### Effect
The character cannot move or act.


## Riding
### Symbol
![condition_ride](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b917dd73-eb59-4cb2-8c80-71692edf3f7f)
### Effect
The character is riding on a steed, effectively moving at the speed of the steed.


## Sleeping
### Symbol
![condition_sleep](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52436846-7a12-44aa-8feb-5a22ec7786b5)
### Effect
The character is asleep, not being able to move or act.


## Sliming
### Symbol
![condition_slime](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/43f57413-c885-42c5-8662-cbb53c8ec925)
### Effect
The character will turn into a green slime in nine turns. This will effectively kill the character and end the game. It's not a polymorph form.


## Slowed
### Symbol
![condition_slow](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2cdd62a2-7a13-449a-b74f-9e07e9b30084)
### Effect
The character's speed is reduced by 1/3.


## Stoning
### Symbol
![condition_stone](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cb609fe8-fa99-4146-b35d-cc2ad9d7bf06)
### Effect
The character will turned into stone in 1 turn.


## Strangulation
### Symbol
![condition_strngl](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0d6a1238-534b-4f93-a468-5f062c1cbaf1)
### Effect
The character will die in 6 turns, unless you are in a non-breathing polymorph form.


## Stun
### Symbol
![condition_stun](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/43e82392-3f75-4ea5-949e-6b7184c6cd7d)
### Effect
The character can't move or act straight. If he or she tries to move or do something requiring a direction, a random direction will be chosen for the action.


## Suffocating
### Symbol
![condition_suffoc](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4955dcfa-63ad-4a77-a11a-af57fa7ec3ce)
### Effect
The character will die in 6 turns.


## Terminal Illness
### Symbol
![condition_termill](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/65efae64-a3a4-4fba-9b7e-05e1fbe1c534)
### Effect
The character will die in 10–19 turns.
