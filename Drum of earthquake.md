## Tool: Drum

- **Weight:** 1.6 lbs
- **Base value:** 25 gold
- **Base material:** Leather
- **Saving throw:** Against wisdom
- **Charges:** Yes
- **Maximum charges:** 10
- **Recharging type:** Rechargeable as musical instrument

## Description

When improvised, playing causes an earthquake in the dungeon.
Also frightens nearby monsters.
