In GnollHack, you can choose one of the following 13 character classes:

* [[/Roles/Archaeologist]] — Lightly equipped explorer
* [[/Roles/Barbarian]] — Melee warrior with poison resistance
* [[/Roles/Caveman]] — Warrior with a club and a sling
* [[/Roles/Healer]] — Rich healing spell caster
* [[/Roles/Knight]] — Mounted armored melee warrior
* [[/Roles/Monk]] — Martial artist with clerical spells
* [[/Roles/Priest]] — Clerical spell caster and diviner
* [[/Roles/Ranger]] — Archer excelling at ranged combat
* [[/Roles/Rogue]] — Thief who backstabs and uses poison
* [[/Roles/Samurai]] — Warrior for melee and ranged combat
* [[/Roles/Tourist]] — Versatile traveler with many skills
* [[/Roles/Valkyrie]] — Melee warrior with cold resistance
* [[/Roles/Wizard]] — Arcane spell caster
