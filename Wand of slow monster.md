## Wand
- **Weight:** 0.4 lbs
- **Base value:** 150 gold
- **Base material:** Metal
- **Skill:** Wand
- **Wand effect duration:** 1d10+100 rounds
- **Wand effect range:** 60'
- **Saving throw:** Against wisdom
- **Charges:** Yes
- **Maximum charges:** 20
- **Recharging type:** Rechargeable normally as wand

## Description

Causes the target to slow down to two-thirds its normal speed.
