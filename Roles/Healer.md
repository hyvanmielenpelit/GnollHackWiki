## Description

Rich physician, adept at healing spells.

## Allowed Races

- [[/Races/Human]]
- [[/Races/Gnoll]]

## Allowed Genders

- Male
- Female

## Allowed Alignments

- [[/Neutral]]

## Class Traits

- Gains extra score from learnt spells of unrestricted schools
- Special achievement is to successfully cast a healing or abjuration spell of level 9 or higher
