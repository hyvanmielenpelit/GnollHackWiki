## Description

Potent archer, adept at ranged combat.

## Allowed Races

- [[/Races/Human]]
- [[/Races/Elf]]
- [[/Races/Gnoll]]
- [[/Races/Orc]]

## Allowed Genders

- Male
- Female

## Allowed Alignments

- [[/Neutral]]
- [[/Chaotic]]

## Class Traits

- Gains extra score from ranged weapons of at least artifact, elite, or mythic quality
- Special achievement is to attain the level of Grand Master in Bows or Crossbows
