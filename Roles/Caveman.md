> 👉 **The Caveman (or Cavewoman) is a strong warrior who starts with a [[/Monsters/saber-tooth tiger cub]] companion, a club, a sling, and a random beneficial amulet.**

## 📋 Character Creation

| Property | Options |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Allowed Races** | [[/Races/Human]], [[/Races/Dwarf]], [[/Races/Gnoll]] |
| **Allowed Genders** | Male `♂️`, Female `♀️` |
| **Allowed Alignments** | [[/Lawful]] `⚖️`, [[/Neutral]] `☯️` |

## 🧬 Class Traits

- Starts with a [[/Monsters/saber-tooth tiger cub]]
- Can practice cannibalism
- Gains extra score from amulets of life saving
- Special achievement is to attain the level of Grand Master in Bludgeoning Weapons
