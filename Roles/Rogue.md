## Description

Versatile warrior, adept at melee and ranged combat and at disarming traps.

## Allowed Races

- [[/Races/Human]]
- [[/Races/Gnoll]]
- [[/Races/Orc]]

## Allowed Genders

- Male
- Female

## Allowed Alignments

- [[/Chaotic]]

## Class Traits

- Can backstab fleeing monsters
- Gains extra score from gold and gems
- Special achievement is to defeat Croesus
