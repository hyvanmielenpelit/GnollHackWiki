## Description

Powerful spell caster who can specialize in many spell schools.

## Allowed Races

- [[/Races/Human]]
- [[/Races/Elf]]
- [[/Races/Orc]]

## Allowed Genders

- Male
- Female

## Allowed Alignments

- [[/Neutral]]
- [[/Chaotic]]

## Class Traits

- Gains extra score from learnt spells of unrestricted schools
- Special achievement is to successfully cast a spell of level 10 or higher
