## Wand
- **Weight:** 0.4 lbs
- **Base value:** 175 gold
- **Skill:** Wand
- **Wand effect duration:** 4d6 rounds
- **Wand effect range:** 50'
- **Saving throw:** Against wisdom
- **Charges:** Yes
- **Maximum charges:** 8
- **Recharging type:** Rechargeable normally as wand
## Description
Shoots a sleeping ray that makes monsters on its way to fall a sleep.
