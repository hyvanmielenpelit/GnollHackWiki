> 👉 **GnollHack features an immersive, spatialized audio system powered by professional middleware and custom-tailored soundsets.**

## 🔊 Sound Engine Features

- **Large Sound Set** — Over 1,100 unique game sounds.
- **Spatial Sounds** — Sound volume and panning depend dynamically on the distance and obstacles between the sound source and the player character.
- **Movable Sound Sources** — Support for moving audio sources, such as buzzing by killer bees as they travel.
- **Internal Sound Sets** — Custom C-level sound set logic allowing for complex interactions, such as footsteps varying based on worn boots and floor material (e.g., wood, stone, ice).

## 🛠️ Audio Technology & Middleware

- **Professional Audio Middleware** — Sounds are managed and played using [FMOD](https://www.fmod.com/), an industry-standard sound system also used in major commercial titles like *World of Warcraft* and *Celeste*.
