## Weapon Skill

Increases to-hit, damage, and the chance of a critical strike of polearms.

| Skill Level | Skill Slots to Advance | Training to Advance | To-Hit Bonus | Damage Bonus | Critical Strike Chance |
| :---------- | :--------------------: | :-----------------: | :----------: | :----------: | :--------------------: |
| **Unskilled** | | | -4 | -2 | 0% |
| **Basic** | 1 | 20 | 0 | 0 | 5% |
| **Skilled** | 2 | 80 | +4 | +2 | 10% |
| **Expert** | 3 | 180 | +8 | +4 | 20% |
| **Master** | 4 | 320 | +12 | +6 | 35% |
| **Grand Master** | 5 | 500 | +16 | +8 | 50% |
